# Tracking DCs

_Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 244_

**Tracking DCs**

| Ground Surface | DC |
|----------------|----|
| Soft surface such as snow | 10 |
| Dirt or grass | 15 |
| Bare stone | 20 |
| Each day since the creature passed | +5 |
| Creature left a trail such as blood | -5 |
^tracking-dcs
