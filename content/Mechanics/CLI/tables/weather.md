# Weather

_Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 109_

**Weather**

| dice: d20 | Temperature |
|-----------|-------------|
| 1-14 | normal for the season |
| 15-17 | `1d4 × 10` degrees Fahrenheit colder than normal |
| 18-20 | `1d4 × 10` degrees Fahrenheit hotter than normal |
^weather
