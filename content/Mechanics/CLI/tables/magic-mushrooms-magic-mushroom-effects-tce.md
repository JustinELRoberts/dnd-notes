# Magic Mushrooms; Magic Mushroom Effects
*Source: Tasha's Cauldron of Everything p. 166* 

**Magic Mushroom Effects**

| dice: d10 | Effects |
|-----------|---------|
| 1 | The creature's skin turns an unusual color. Roll a `d4`: 1, purple with yellow splotches; 2, bright orange with tiger stripes; 3, tree-frog green with red squiggles; 4, hot pink with yellow spots. This change is permanent unless removed by a [greater restoration](Mechanics/CLI/spells/greater-restoration-xphb.md) spell or similar magic. |
| 2 | The creature gains the enlarge or reduce effect (50 percent chance of either) of the [enlarge/reduce](Mechanics/CLI/spells/enlarge-reduce-xphb.md) spell for 1 hour. |
| 3 | The creature regains `5d8 + 20` hit points. |
| 4 | Vocally, the creature can only cluck and croon like a chicken. The creature can also understand and speak to chickens. This curse lasts for 1 hour unless ended by a [remove curse](Mechanics/CLI/spells/remove-curse-xphb.md) spell or similar magic. |
| 5 | The creature can understand and speak all languages for `1d4` days. |
| 6 | The creature gains the benefits of the [telepathy](Mechanics/CLI/spells/telepathy-xphb.md) spell for the next 24 hours. |
| 7 | The creature gains the benefits of the [speak with plants](Mechanics/CLI/spells/speak-with-plants-xphb.md) spell for 8 hours. |
| 8 | The creature immediately casts the [time stop](Mechanics/CLI/spells/time-stop-xphb.md) spell, requiring no components. Constitution is the spellcasting ability for this spell. |
| 9 | The creature immediately casts the [detect thoughts](Mechanics/CLI/spells/detect-thoughts-xphb.md) spell, requiring no components. Constitution is the spellcasting ability for this spell. |
| 10 | Magical mists pour out of the creature's eyes and ears, acting as a [fog cloud](Mechanics/CLI/spells/fog-cloud-xphb.md) spell for 1 hour that is centered on the creature and moves with it. |
