# Research Outcomes

_Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything p. 132_

**Research Outcomes**

| Check Total | Outcome |
|-------------|---------|
| 1—5 | No effect. |
| 6—10 | You learn one piece of lore. |
| 11—20 | You learn two pieces of lore. |
| 21+ | You learn three pieces of lore. |
