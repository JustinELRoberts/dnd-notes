# Indifferent [Attitude]
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 369. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)* 

An Indifferent creature has no desire to help or hinder you. Indifferent is the default attitude of a [monster](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/monster-xphb.md).
