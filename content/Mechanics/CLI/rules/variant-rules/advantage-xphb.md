# Advantage

_Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 360. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)_

If you have Advantage on a [D20 Test](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md), roll two d20s, and use the higher roll. A roll can't be affected by more than one Advantage, and Advantage and [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on the same roll cancel each other.
