# Unseen Artisan

_2nd-level, Conjuration (ritual)_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action unless cast as a ritual
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pouch containing 5+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

This spell creates an [Invisible](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Invisible), mindless, shapeless, Medium force that performs specialized tasks at your command until the spell ends. The artisan springs into existence in an unoccupied space on the ground within range. It has AC 10, 1 [Hit Point](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), and a Strength of 10, and it can't attack. If it drops to 0 [Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), the spell ends.

Once on each of your turns as a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can mentally command the artisan to take the [Utilize](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Utilize) action with a set of tools within range or to craft a nonmagical item. The artisan has proficiency with all [Artisan's Tools](/Mechanics/CLI/items/artisans-tools-xphb.md) and can perform tasks that a skilled human worker could do. You must provide the artisan with the tools and necessary raw materials for its work. If the artisan must make an ability check, it uses your spellcasting ability for the ability check.

If you command the artisan to perform a task that would move it more than 60 feet away from you, the spell ends.
