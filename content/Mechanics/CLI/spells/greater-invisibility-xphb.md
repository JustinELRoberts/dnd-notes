# Greater Invisibility

_4th-level, Illusion_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A creature you touch has the [Invisible](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Invisible) condition until the spell ends.
