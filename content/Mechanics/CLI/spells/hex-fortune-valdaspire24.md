# Hex: Fortune
*cantrip, Divination*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends at the end of your next turn, the target has [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws.
