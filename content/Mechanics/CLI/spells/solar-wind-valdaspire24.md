# Solar Wind
*cantrip, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A gust of solar radiance bombards a creature that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take `1d8` Radiant damage and be pushed 10 feet away from you.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `1d8` when you reach levels 5 (`2d8`), 11 (`3d8`), and 17 (`4d8`).
