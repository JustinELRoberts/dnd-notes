# Hex: Bleeding

_cantrip, Necromancy_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

One creature you can see within range makes a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, each time the target takes damage until the spell ends at the end of your next turn, it takes an extra `1d4` Necrotic damage. If the target is [Bloodied](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Bloodied), it takes an extra `1d6` Necrotic damage instead.
