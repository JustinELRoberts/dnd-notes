# Skirmish
*3rd-level, Transmutation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a branch from a tree)
- **Duration:** 1 minute

You enhance the mobility of one willing creature you touch, enabling them to move easily through enemy formations. For the duration, the target's [Speed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is doubled, its movement is unaffected by [Difficult Terrain](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/difficult-terrain-xphb.md), and it doesn't provoke [Opportunity Attacks](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack).
