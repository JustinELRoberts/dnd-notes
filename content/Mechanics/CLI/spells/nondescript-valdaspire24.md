# Nondescript

_2nd-level, Enchantment_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a paper bag)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

This spell makes you seem unremarkable, though it doesn't change your actual appearance. Creatures that see or hear you for the duration are unable to recall your appearance or voice.
