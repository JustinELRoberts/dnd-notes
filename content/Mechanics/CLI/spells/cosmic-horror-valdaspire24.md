# Cosmic Horror
*3rd-level, Conjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a dream journal)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

For the briefest moment, you open a portal to an eldritch dimension in a 10-foot-radius [Sphere](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) centered on a point you can see within range. Each creature within the [Sphere](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) makes a Wisdom saving throw as it beholds the nightmarish dimension. On a failed save, a creature takes `6d6` Psychic damage and has the [Frightened](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Frightened) condition for the duration. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage only.

A creature [Frightened](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Frightened) by this spell makes another Wisdom saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `1d6` for each spell slot level above 3.
