# Regenerate

_7th-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a prayer wheel)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

A creature you touch regains `4d8 + 15` [Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). For the duration, the target regains 1 [Hit Point](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) at the start of each of its turns, and any severed body parts regrow after 2 minutes.
