# Divine Favor

_1st-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 minute

Until the spell ends, your attacks with weapons deal an extra `1d4` Radiant damage on a hit.
