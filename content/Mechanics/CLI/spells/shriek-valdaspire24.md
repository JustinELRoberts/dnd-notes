# Shriek
*cantrip, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self (5-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You release a blood-curdling screech in a 5-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md). Each Large or smaller creature you choose within the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) is pushed 5 feet away from you.

**Classes**: [Warlock](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-warlock.md); [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0*
