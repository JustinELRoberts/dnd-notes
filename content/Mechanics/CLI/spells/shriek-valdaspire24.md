# Shriek

_cantrip, Evocation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self (5-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You release a blood-curdling screech in a 5-foot [Emanation](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md). Each Large or smaller creature you choose within the [Emanation](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) is pushed 5 feet away from you.
