# Blunder

_2nd-level, Enchantment_

- **Casting time:** 1 Reaction
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** S, M (a banana peel)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

The attacker must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or suffer one of the following effects of your choice:

## Fumble

The target drops one object of your choice that it's holding, with the object landing in a space you choose up to 10 feet away from the target. If the target drops the weapon it is using for the attack roll, it can make an [Unarmed Strike](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md) for the triggering attack.

## Prat Fall

The target has the [Prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) condition. The [Disadvantage](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) from the [Prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) condition applies against the triggering attack, potentially causing it to miss.
