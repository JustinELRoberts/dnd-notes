# Jethro's Instant Reload

_2nd-level, Conjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a spent bullet casing)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

One Ranged weapon you touch becomes enchanted to reload itself automatically. If the weapon has the Cooldown, [Loading](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Loading), or [Reload](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Reload), you ignore the property for the duration. When the weapon's ammunition is depleted, ammunition you are carrying teleports into the weapon.
