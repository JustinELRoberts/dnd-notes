# Action
*1st-level, Transmutation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a shaving of licorice root)
- **Duration:** 1 round

Choose a willing creature that you can see within range. During its next turn, the target gains an additional action. That action can be used only to take the [Attack](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Attack) (one weapon attack only), [Dash](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Dash), [Disengage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Disengage), [Hide](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Hide), or [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action.
