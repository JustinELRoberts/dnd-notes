# Power Word Heal

_9th-level, Enchantment_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A wave of healing energy washes over one creature you can see within range. The target regains all its [Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). If the creature has the [Charmed](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Charmed), [Frightened](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Frightened), [Paralyzed](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Paralyzed), [Poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned), or [Stunned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Stunned) condition, the condition ends. If the creature has the [Prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) condition, it can use its [Reaction](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to stand up.
