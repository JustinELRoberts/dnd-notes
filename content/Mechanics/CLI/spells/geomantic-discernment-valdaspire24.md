# Geomantic Discernment

_3rd-level, Divination (ritual)_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action unless cast as a ritual
- **Range:** Self (60-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

You gain the ability to sense valuable minerals in a 60-foot [Emanation](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you. A glowing aura that only you can see appears around valuable metals, gems, ores, native elements, or other resources in that [Emanation](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) for the spell's duration. You can see this aura through any amount of nonmagical material.
