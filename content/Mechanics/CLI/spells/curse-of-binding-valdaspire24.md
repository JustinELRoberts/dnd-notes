# Curse of Binding

_7th-level, Enchantment (ritual)_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action unless cast as a ritual
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a body part from the intended target, such as a fingernail, a lock of hair, or a drop of blood)
- **Duration:** Until dispelled

You attempt to bind a creature or object to a location. Choose a target and a point within range that you can see. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be cursed to remain within 30 feet of the point. If the target starts its turn further than 30 feet from the point, it must succeed on a Strength ([Athletics](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Athletics)) check against your spell save DC or be dragged 60 feet toward the point. Additionally, if the creature tries to use teleportation or interplanar travel, it must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failure, the attempt fails and wastes the spell or effect. On a success, the spell ends.

**Classes**: [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

_Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0_
