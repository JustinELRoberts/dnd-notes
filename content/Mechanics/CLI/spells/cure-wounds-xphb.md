# Cure Wounds

_1st-level, Abjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A creature you touch regains a number of [Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to `2d8` plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The healing increases by `2d8` for each spell slot level above 1.
