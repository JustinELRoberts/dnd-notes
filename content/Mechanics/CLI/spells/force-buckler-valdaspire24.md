# Force Buckler

_cantrip, Abjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a specially-prepared gauntlet worth 5+ GP)
- **Duration:** 1 round

You summon a translucent field of force, which springs forth from the gauntlet used in the spell's casting. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +2 bonus to your [Armor Class](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) while you aren't wielding a [Shield](/Mechanics/CLI/items/shield-xphb.md). This spell ends early if you are hit by an attack.
