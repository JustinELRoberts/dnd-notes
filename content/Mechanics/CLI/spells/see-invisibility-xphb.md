# See Invisibility

_2nd-level, Divination_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of talc)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

For the duration, you see creatures and objects that have the [Invisible](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Invisible) condition as if they were visible, and you can see into the Ethereal Plane. Creatures and objects there appear ghostly.
