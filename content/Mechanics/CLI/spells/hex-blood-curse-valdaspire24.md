# Hex: Blood Curse
*cantrip, Transmutation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

A creature you can see within range becomes cursed until the spell ends at the end of your next turn. If the target takes damage while cursed and has fewer than 5 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) after taking damage, it drops to 0 [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md).

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The threshold of [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) under which the target drops to 0 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) increases by 5 when you reach levels 5 (10 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md)), 11 (15 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md)), and 17 (20 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md)).
