# Minor Lifesteal
*cantrip, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You attempt to drain life energy from a creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take `1d6` Necrotic damage. You gain [Temporary Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the amount of Necrotic damage dealt.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `1d6` when you reach levels 5 (`2d6`), 11 (`3d6`), and 17 (`4d6`).
