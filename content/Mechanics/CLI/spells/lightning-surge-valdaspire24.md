# Lightning Surge

_cantrip, Evocation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (5-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S, M (two bits of copper wire)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A dazzling array of lightning bolts erupts from you. Each creature within a 5-foot [Emanation](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take `1d6` Lightning damage.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `1d6` when you reach levels 5 (`2d6`), 11 (`3d6`), and 17 (`4d6`).
