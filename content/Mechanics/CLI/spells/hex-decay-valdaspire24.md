# Hex: Decay

_cantrip, Necromancy_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

One creature you can see within range must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take `1d6` Necrotic damage. This damage ignores [Resistance](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) and [Immunity](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/immunity-xphb.md). The creature takes this damage again at the end of each of its turns. The spell ends at the end of your next turn.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `1d6` when you reach levels 5 (`2d6`), 11 (`3d6`), and 17 (`4d6`).
