# Hex: Stupor
*cantrip, Enchantment*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 10 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

One creature you can see within range makes a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target has the [Incapacitated](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition until the spell ends at the end of your next turn. The spell ends on a target if it takes damage or someone within 5 feet of it takes an action to shake it out of the spell's effect. Once the spell ends on a target, it is immune to this spell for the next 24 hours.

**Classes**: [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0*
