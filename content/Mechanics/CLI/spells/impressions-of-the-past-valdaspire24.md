# Impressions of the Past

_7th-level, Divination_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (an hourglass and a glass eye worth 100+ GP)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You cast your senses back in time to perceive your current location as it was at some point in the past, including any events that were happening at that time. You must specify the exact time you wish to see, which can't be more than 100 years ago. The past appears dreamlike and shadowy. While viewing the past, you can look in any direction, discern detail, and hear speech as normal, but you can't move or speak.
