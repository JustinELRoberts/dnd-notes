# Prehensile Hair
*1st-level, Transmutation (ritual)*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action unless cast as a ritual
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a braided rope)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

When you cast this spell, you can cause any of your body hair to grow up to 15 feet in length. Your hair becomes supernaturally tough and you can magically use it to extend your reach for the duration. Using your hair, you can cast spells with a range of Touch, take the [Help](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Help) or [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action, or perform simple tasks out to a range of 15 feet. Your hair can't wield weapons, [Shields](Mechanics/CLI/items/shield-xphb.md), or carry objects heavier than 10 pounds. Your hair returns to its normal length when this spell ends.

**Classes**: [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0*
