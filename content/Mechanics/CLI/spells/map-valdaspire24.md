# Map

_2nd-level, Divination (ritual)_

- **Casting time:** 1 minute unless cast as a ritual
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (Ink Pen, Paper, and ink worth 10+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

This spell magically generates a gridded [Map](/Mechanics/CLI/items/map-xphb.md) of an area you choose within range. The Map shows a 50-foot Square area on a 5-foot grid and includes scale-accurate details of non-moving objects in that area. The map is rendered without color, and doesn't include secret doors, traps, or creatures. In a multi-level structure, the map only details the level on which the spell is cast.
