# Hex: Misfortune

_cantrip, Divination_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

Choose one creature you can see within range. Until the spell ends at the end of your next turn, whenever the target rolls a 20 on the `d20` of a [D20 Test](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md), the roll instead becomes a 1.
