# Beacon of Hope

_3rd-level, Abjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Choose any number of creatures within range. For the duration, each target has [Advantage](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Wisdom saving throws and [Death Saving Throws](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/death-saving-throw-xphb.md) and regains the maximum number of [Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) possible from any healing.
