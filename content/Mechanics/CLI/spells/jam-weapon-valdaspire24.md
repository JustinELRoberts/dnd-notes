# Jam Weapon
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Reaction
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of wet gunpowder)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

The weapon you target suffers a malfunction and the attack fails. A malfunctioning weapon can't be used to make an attack until a creature takes the [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action to fix the weapon malfunction.
