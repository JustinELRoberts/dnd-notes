# After Image
*3rd-level, Illusion*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a silver hand mirror worth 50+ GP)
- **Duration:** 10 minutes

You create an illusory duplicate of yourself which follows your every movement. When a creature hits you with an attack roll while your duplicate remains, roll a `d6`. If it rolls a 3 or higher, the duplicate is hit instead of you and the duplicate is destroyed. The duplicate otherwise ignores all other damage and effects. The duplicate reappears if you move 15 feet or more on your turn or take the [Dodge](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Dodge) action.

A creature is unaffected by this spell if it has the [Blinded](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Blinded) condition, [Blindsight](Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Blindsight), or [Truesight](Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Truesight).
