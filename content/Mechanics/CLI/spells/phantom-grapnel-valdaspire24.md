# Phantom Grapnel

_cantrip, Evocation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You conjure a chain and hook made of magical force, which you propel at a creature or unoccupied space you can see within range. When you target a space or a creature of Huge size or larger, your grapnel pulls you to that target in a straight line. You provoke [Opportunity Attacks](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) for this movement as normal. When you target a creature of Large size or smaller, the target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 10 feet toward you.
