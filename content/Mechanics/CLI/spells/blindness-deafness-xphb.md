# Blindness/Deafness

_2nd-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** 1 minute

One creature that you can see within range must succeed on a Constitution saving throw, or it has the [Blinded](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Blinded) or [Deafened](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Deafened) condition (your choice) for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target repeats the save, ending the spell on itself on a success.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 2.
