# Stoneskin

_4th-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch has [Resistance](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.
