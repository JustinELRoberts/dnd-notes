# Delay

_1st-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (an octagonal sign)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You briefly slow time for a creature of your choice that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be moved to last place in the [Initiative](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) order from the start of the next round onwards.
