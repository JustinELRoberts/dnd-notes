# Hex: Mire

_cantrip, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You transform the ground in a 30-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you into murky swamp, which is [Difficult Terrain](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/difficult-terrain-xphb.md) until the spell ends at the end of your next turn. This [Difficult Terrain](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/difficult-terrain-xphb.md) doesn't cost you extra movement.

**Classes**: [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

_Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0_
