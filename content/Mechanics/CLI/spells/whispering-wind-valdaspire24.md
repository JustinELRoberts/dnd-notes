# Whispering Wind
*1st-level, Evocation (ritual)*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action unless cast as a ritual
- **Range:** 1 miles
- **Components:** V, S, M (a feather)
- **Duration:** 1 round

You whisper a message of twenty-five words or less, while specifying up to six creatures to be the targets. If a target is in range, it can hear the message carried on the wind and can reply in a whisper that only you can hear.

The message is blocked if every path within range to the target is blocked by solid objects or areas of magical silence.
