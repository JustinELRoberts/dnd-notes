# Darkvision

_2nd-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a dried carrot)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

For the duration, a willing creature you touch has [Darkvision](/Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Darkvision) with a range of 150 feet.
