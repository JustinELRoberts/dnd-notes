# Hex: Doomward

_cantrip, Abjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You touch a willing creature. The first time the target would drop to 0 [Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) before the spell ends at the end of your next turn, the target instead drops to 1 [Hit Point](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), and the spell ends. This spell has no effect if you have cast this spell on the target within the past 24 hours.
