# Phantasmal Beauty

_3rd-level, Illusion_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You tap into the dreams of a creature you can see within range and create an illusory manifestation of its deepest desires, visible only to that creature. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target has the [Charmed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Charmed) and [Incapacitated](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) conditions and a [Speed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) of 0 for the duration.

The target makes a Wisdom saving throw whenever it takes damage and at the end of each of its turns for the duration. On a successful save, it takes `4d10` Psychic damage and the spell ends.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `1d10` for each spell slot level above 4.
