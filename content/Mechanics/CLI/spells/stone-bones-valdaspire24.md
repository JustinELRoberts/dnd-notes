# Stone Bones

_2nd-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

Choose a creature you can see within range. The target has [Resistance](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage until the spell ends at the end of your next turn.
