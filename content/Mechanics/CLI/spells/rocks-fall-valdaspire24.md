# Rocks Fall

_8th-level, Conjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (ten dice)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Boulders appear in the air and fall in a 60-foot-radius, 120-foot-high [Cylinder](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/cylinder-area-of-effect-xphb.md) centered on a point within range. Each creature in the [Cylinder](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/cylinder-area-of-effect-xphb.md) makes a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a target takes `10d8` Bludgeoning damage and is buried under the rubble. On a successful save, a target takes half as much damage only.

A buried creature has the [Prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) and [Restrained](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Restrained) conditions, and can take an action to make a Strength ([Athletics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Athletics)) check against your spell save DC, freeing itself from the boulders on a success and is no longer [Prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) or [Restrained](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Restrained) by them.
