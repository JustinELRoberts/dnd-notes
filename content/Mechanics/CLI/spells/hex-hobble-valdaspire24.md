# Hex: Hobble
*cantrip, Transmutation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

One creature you can see within range makes a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, the creature's [Speed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is reduced to 10 feet until the spell ends at the end of your next turn. If the target is flying, it falls, unless it has the ability to hover.

**Classes**: [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0*
