# Hex: Duplicity

_cantrip, Illusion_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You create an illusory duplicate of yourself in your space. When a creature hits you with an attack before the spell ends at the end of your next turn, roll a `d6`. On 4 or higher, the attack strikes the duplicate instead of you and the spell ends. The duplicate otherwise ignore all other damage and effects. A creature is unaffected by this spell if it has the [Blinded](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Blinded) condition, [Blindsight](Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Blindsight), or [Truesight](Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Truesight).

**Classes**: [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

_Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0_
