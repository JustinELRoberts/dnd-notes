# Hex: Duplicity

_cantrip, Illusion_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You create an illusory duplicate of yourself in your space. When a creature hits you with an attack before the spell ends at the end of your next turn, roll a `d6`. On 4 or higher, the attack strikes the duplicate instead of you and the spell ends. The duplicate otherwise ignore all other damage and effects. A creature is unaffected by this spell if it has the [Blinded](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Blinded) condition, [Blindsight](/Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Blindsight), or [Truesight](/Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Truesight).
