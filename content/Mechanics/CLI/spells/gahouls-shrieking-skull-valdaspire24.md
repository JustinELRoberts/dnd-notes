# Gahoul's Shrieking Skull

_1st-level, Necromancy_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a skull)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You throw a cackling skull at a point you can see within range, which emits a mortifying shriek audible out to 300 feet. Each creature in a 10-foot [Cube](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/cube-area-of-effect-xphb.md) takes `3d4` Thunder damage, without making a saving throw.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `1d4` for each spell slot level above 1.
