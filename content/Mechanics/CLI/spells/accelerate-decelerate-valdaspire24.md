# Accelerate/Decelerate

_1st-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Reaction
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of oil or molasses)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

This spell accelerates or decelerates an attack (see the chosen effect below) the instant before it strikes, lessening or multiplying its force.

## Accelerate

The target takes an extra `2d6` damage from the attack. This extra damage is the same type dealt by the triggering attack.

## Decelerate

Reduce the damage the target takes by `2d6` (to a minimum of 0 damage).

## Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot

The extra damage or reduction in damage increases by `1d6` for each slot level above 1.
