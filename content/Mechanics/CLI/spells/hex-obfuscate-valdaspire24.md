# Hex: Obfuscate

_cantrip, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (20-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You produce fog in a 20-foot [Emanation](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you. The fog spreads around corners, and its area is [Heavily Obscured](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/heavily-obscured-xphb.md). This fog lasts until the spell ends at the end of your next turn or until a strong wind (such as one created by [Gust of Wind](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/gust-of-wind-xphb.md)) disperses it, ending the spell.
