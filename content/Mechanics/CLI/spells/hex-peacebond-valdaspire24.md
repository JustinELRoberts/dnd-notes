# Hex: Peacebond
*cantrip, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You create a 30-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you that locks weapons to their containers. The weapons and ammunition of each creature within the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) become fixed in their sheaths, quivers, or holsters until the spell ends at the end of your next turn. A creature can take a [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action to make a Strength ([Athletics](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Athletics)) check against your spell save DC, freeing an item from its container on a success.

**Classes**: [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0*
