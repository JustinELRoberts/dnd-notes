# Hex: Peacebond

_cantrip, Abjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You create a 30-foot [Emanation](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you that locks weapons to their containers. The weapons and ammunition of each creature within the [Emanation](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) become fixed in their sheaths, quivers, or holsters until the spell ends at the end of your next turn. A creature can take a [Utilize](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Utilize) action to make a Strength ([Athletics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Athletics)) check against your spell save DC, freeing an item from its container on a success.
