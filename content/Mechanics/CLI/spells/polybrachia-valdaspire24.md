# Polybrachia

_3rd-level, Conjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pair of armbands)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Two muscular arms of brilliant arcane energy appear on a willing creature that you touch. These arms are fully functional and can be used to wield weapons and Shields (allowing the target to simultaneously hold 2 two-handed weapons, or 4 one-handed weapons), perform Somatic components of spells, and perform other actions. For the duration, the target has [Advantage](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Strength ([Athletics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Athletics)) checks. The target can take a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to make a melee attack using a weapon wielded by the arms.
