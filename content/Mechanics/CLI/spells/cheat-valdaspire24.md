# Cheat

_cantrip, Divination_

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** S, M (a weighted die)
- **Duration:** 1 round

You twist your fingers and fate seems to follow suit. Until the end of your next turn, you can reroll any ability check you make to play nonmagical games of skill. Therefore, this spell could influence a game of poker, but not the result of a Deck of Many Things.
