# Hex: Evil Eye

_cantrip, Illusion_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

One creature you can see within range makes a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target has the [Frightened](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Frightened) condition until the spell ends at the end of your next turn.
