# Hex: Vanish

_cantrip, Illusion_

- **Casting time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

Repeating an ancient chant, you have the [Invisible](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Invisible) condition until the spell ends at the end of your next turn. Creatures within 20 feet of yourself that can hear can detect your location for the spell's duration. The spell ends early if you make an attack roll, deal damage, or cast a spell.
