# Curse Ward

_1st-level, Abjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 8 hours

You reach out your hand and touch a willing creature, raising a smoke-like barrier around it. For the duration, the target has [Resistance](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Necrotic damage and can't be cursed or possessed. If the target is already under one of these effects, the effect is suppressed until the spell ends.
