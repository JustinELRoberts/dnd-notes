# Antiballistics Field

_6th-level, Abjuration_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (40-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of wet gunpowder)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

A 40-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) extends from you, disrupting projectiles and causing Ranged weapons to malfunction. Within the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md), whenever a Ranged weapon is used for an attack, the weapon immediately malfunctions and the attack is lost. A malfunctioning weapon can't be used to make an attack until a creature takes the [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action to fix the weapon malfunction. Ranged attacks using weapons whose projectiles pass through the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) have [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) and deal only half damage on a hit.

**Classes**: [Bard](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Necromancer](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-necromancer.md); [Wizard (Abjurer)](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-abjurer-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Wizard](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-wizard.md)
