# Intensify Gravity

_4th-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 100 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a lodestone and iron filings)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

This spell intensifies gravity in a 50-foot-radius, 100-foot-high [Cylinder](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/cylinder-area-of-effect-xphb.md) centered on a point within range.

A creature in the area must spend 2 feet of movement for every 1 foot it moves. Combined with [Difficult Terrain](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/difficult-terrain-xphb.md), a creature must spend 3 feet of movement for every 1 foot it moves. If a creature with the [Prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) condition attempts to right itself, it must succeed on a Strength check against your spell save DC. On a failure, its [Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is 0 until the end of the current turn.

Objects in the area, including those being worn or carried, weigh double their usual weight. This may cause creatures to become encumbered or cause spells with a weight limit, such as [Levitate](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/levitate-xphb.md), to fail.

The [Fly Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/fly-speed-xphb.md) of a creature in an area of high gravity is reduced to 0.

Lastly, creatures and objects take double damage from falling.
