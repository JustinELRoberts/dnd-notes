# Restore Youth

_3rd-level, Transmutation (ritual)_

- **Casting time:** 8 hours unless cast as a ritual
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (gold dust worth 500+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You perform a complex ritual for a creature you touch, reducing its apparent age by `2d10` years, to a minimum of 18 years. This spell doesn't extend the creature's lifespan.
