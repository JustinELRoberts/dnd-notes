# Hunger of the Earth

_4th-level, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

The ground opens up beneath a creature you choose on the ground within range. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pulled underground for the duration. On a successful save, its [Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is 0 until the start of your next turn.

A creature pulled underground has the [Blinded](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Blinded) and [Restrained](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Restrained) conditions, can't teleport, has [Total Cover](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/cover-xphb.md) against attacks and other effects outside the ground, and takes `4d6` Bludgeoning damage at the end of each of its turns. At the end of each of its turns, the target repeats the save, ending the spell on itself on a success.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 4.
