# Hex: Disorient

_cantrip, Necromancy_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

One creature you can see within range makes a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target must subtract `1d4` from attack rolls it makes until the spell ends at the end of your next turn.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The die subtracted from attack rolls changes when you reach levels 5 (`1d6`), 11 (`1d8`), and 17 (`1d10`).
