# Hex: Tremors
*cantrip, Transmutation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (5-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

A miniature earthquake shakes the ground in a 5-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you. Each creature on the ground in the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or have the [Prone](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition. A creature that enters the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) or ends its turn there must also succeed on that save or fall [Prone](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Prone). This spell ends at the end of your next turn.

**Classes**: [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0*
