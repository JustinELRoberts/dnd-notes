# Force Weapon

_cantrip, Evocation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 5 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a specially-prepared gauntlet worth 5+ GP)
- **Duration:** 1 round

You conjure a blade of magical force in the air, which strikes at a creature within range. Make a melee spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes `1d10` Force damage. The blade remains in existence for a short time; until the start of your next turn, you can make a single strike with your mystical blade as an [Opportunity Attack](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack).

**Cantrip Upgrade.** You make an additional attack when you reach levels 5 (2 attacks), 11 (3 attacks), and 17 (4 attacks). These attacks can target the same creature or different ones.
