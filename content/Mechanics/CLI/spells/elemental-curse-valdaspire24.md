# Elemental Curse

_6th-level, Evocation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A spark leaps from your finger to a creature you touch, spreading in bright cracks across its skin. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be cursed for the duration. The cursed target has [Vulnerability](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/vulnerability-xphb.md) to your choice of [Acid](/Mechanics/CLI/items/acid-xphb.md), Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder damage. If the creature has [Resistance](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to the chosen damage type, it instead loses that [Resistance](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/resistance-xphb.md). If the target has [Immunity](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/immunity-xphb.md) to the chosen damage type, it instead has [Resistance](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to that damage type.
