# Hex: Sweet Tooth

_cantrip, Transmutation_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You touch a nonmagical object within range that isn't being worn or carried and that can fit within a 1-foot [Cube](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cube-area-of-effect-xphb.md). It transforms into an edible candy replica. You choose the type of candy it is made out of, such as gingerbread, candy cane, licorice, or a combination of various candies. If the candy is not eaten or destroyed when the spell ends at the end of your next turn, it reverts to its original material. Once the spell ends on a target, it is immune to this spell for 1 minute.
