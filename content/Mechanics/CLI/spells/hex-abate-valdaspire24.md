# Hex: Abate

_cantrip, Enchantment_

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

One creature you can see within range makes a Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, the target can't make [Opportunity Attacks](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) until the spell ends at the end of your next turn.

**Classes**: [Witch](Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-witch.md)

_Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024) p. 0_
