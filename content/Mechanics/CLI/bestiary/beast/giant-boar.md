## Traits

***Charge.*** If the boar moves at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a tusk attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 7 (`2d6`) slashing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked [prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone).

***Relentless (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest).*** If the boar takes 10 damage or less that would reduce it to 0 hit points, it is reduced to 1 hit point instead.

## Actions
