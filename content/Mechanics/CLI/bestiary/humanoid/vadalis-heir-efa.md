# Vadalis Heir

![](Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/img/vadalis-heir.webp#right)
Combining druidic magic with skill at handling natural and monstrous animals, the dragonmarked heirs of House Vadalis, most of whom are human, are more fearsome than their innocuous-seeming house suggests—especially when accompanied by a Giant Boar, Polar Bear, or Rhinoceros.

## Traits

***Pack Tactics.*** The heir has [Advantage](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the heir's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn't have the [Incapacitated](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The heir makes four attacks, using Scimitar or Lunar Wisp in any combination.

***Scimitar.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* `+5`, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 6 (`1d6 + 3`) Slashing damage plus 3 (`1d6`) Poison damage.

***Lunar Wisp.*** *Ranged Attack Roll:* `+5`, range 60 ft. *Hit:* 9 (`2d8`) Radiant damage, and the target emits [Dim Light](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) in a 10-foot radius and can't benefit from the [Invisible](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Invisible) condition until the end of the heir's next turn.

***Spellcasting.*** The heir casts one of the following spells, using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13):

**At will:** [Druidcraft](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/druidcraft-xphb.md), [Light](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/light-xphb.md), [Speak with Animals](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/speak-with-animals-xphb.md)

**3/day each:** [Animal Friendship](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/animal-friendship-xphb.md), [Animal Messenger](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/animal-messenger-xphb.md)

**1/day:** [Ice Storm](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/ice-storm-xphb.md)

## Bonus Actions

***Spur Beast.*** The heir targets a Beast it can see within 30 feet. The target can take a [Reaction](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to move up to half its [Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) and make one melee attack.
