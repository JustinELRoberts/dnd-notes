# Scoundrel

Stealthy and subversive, the scoundrel is as adept an assassin as a burglar or thief.

- **Proficiency Bonus** +2

- **Skills** [Acrobatics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Acrobatics) +5, [Perception](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Perception) +2, [Sleight of Hand](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Sleight%20of%20Hand) +5, [Stealth](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Stealth) +5
- **Senses** passive Perception 12
- **Languages** Common plus one other language
- **Challenge** 

## Traits

***Proficiencies.*** Simple weapons, Martial weapons that have the [Finesse](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Finesse) or [Light](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Light) property; Light armor

***Level 5.*** Martial Excellence. The scoundrel has a +1 bonus to its attack and damage rolls. This bonus increases to +2 at Captain level 9, and +3 at Captain level 13.

***Level 17.*** Assassinate (1/Day). During the first round of combat, the scoundrel turns a hit that it makes into a [Critical Hit](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/critical-hit-xphb.md).

## Actions

***Shortsword.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* Bonus equals your Charisma modifier plus your [Proficiency Bonus](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), reach 5 ft. *Hit:* `1d6` plus your Charisma modifier Piercing damage.

***Shortbow.*** *Ranged Attack Roll:* Bonus equals your Charisma modifier plus your [Proficiency Bonus](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), range 80/320 ft. *Hit:* `1d6` plus your Charisma modifier Piercing damage.

## Bonus Actions

***Cunning Action.*** The scoundrel takes the [Dash](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Dash), [Disengage](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Disengage), or [Hide](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Hide) action.

***Level 5.*** Improved Cunning [Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/action-xphb.md). The scoundrel takes the [Dodge](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Dodge) or [Help](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Help) action.

***Level 9.*** Poison Strike. The scoundrel deals an extra `2d6` Poison damage on a hit.

## Reactions

***Level 13.*** Uncanny Dodge. Trigger: The scoundrel is hit by an attack roll. _Response:_ The scoundrel halves the damage (round down).
