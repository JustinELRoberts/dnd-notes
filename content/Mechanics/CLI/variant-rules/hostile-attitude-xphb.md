# Hostile \[Attitude]

_Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 368. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)_

A Hostile creature views you unfavorably. You have [Disadvantage](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on an ability check to influence a Hostile creature.
