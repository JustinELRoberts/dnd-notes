# Attack Roll

_Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 361. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)_

An attack roll is a [D20 Test](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md) that represents making an attack with a weapon, an [Unarmed Strike](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md), or a spell.
