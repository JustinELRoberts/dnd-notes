# Temporary Hit Points

_Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 376. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)_

Temporary Hit Points are granted by certain effects and act as a buffer against losing real [Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md).
