# Boon of the Fire Soul

You have immunity to fire damage. You can also cast [burning hands](Mechanics/CLI/spells/burning-hands-xphb.md) (save DC 15) at will, without using a spell slot or any components.

## Sources

_Dungeon Master's Guide p. 232_
