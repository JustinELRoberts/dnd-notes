# Charm of Dhakaan


While you have this Charm, you know the Goblin language. You can expend the Charm to cast [Staggering Smite](Mechanics/CLI/spells/staggering-smite-xphb.md).

## Sources

*Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 51*
