# Boon of the Unfettered

You have advantage on ability checks made to resist being [grappled](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Grappled). In addition, you can use an action to automatically escape a grapple or free yourself of restraints of any kind.

## Sources

_Dungeon Master's Guide p. 232_
