# Manifest Charm of Lamannia, the Twilight Forest
*Charm*  


When you use a spell slot to cast a spell that conjures or summons a Beast, an Elemental, or a Fey (including [Conjure Animals](Mechanics/CLI/spells/conjure-animals-xphb.md), [Conjure Elemental](Mechanics/CLI/spells/conjure-elemental-xphb.md), [Conjure Fey](Mechanics/CLI/spells/conjure-fey-xphb.md), [Conjure Minor Elementals](Mechanics/CLI/spells/conjure-minor-elementals-xphb.md), [Conjure Woodland Beings](Mechanics/CLI/spells/conjure-woodland-beings-xphb.md), [Find Steed](Mechanics/CLI/spells/find-steed-xphb.md), [Summon Beast](Mechanics/CLI/spells/summon-beast-xphb.md), [Summon Elemental](Mechanics/CLI/spells/summon-elemental-xphb.md), or [Summon Fey](Mechanics/CLI/spells/summon-fey-xphb.md)), you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.
