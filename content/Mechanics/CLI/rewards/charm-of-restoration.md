# Charm of Restoration


This charm has 6 charges. You can use an action to expend some of its charges to cast one of the following spells: [greater restoration](Mechanics/CLI/spells/greater-restoration-xphb.md) (4 charges) or [lesser restoration](Mechanics/CLI/spells/lesser-restoration-xphb.md) (2 charges). Once all its charges have been expended, the charm vanishes from you.

## Sources

*Dungeon Master's Guide p. 228*
