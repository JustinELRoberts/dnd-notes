# Charm of the Slayer

One sword in your possession becomes a [dragon slayer](/Mechanics/CLI/items/dragon-slayer.md) or [giant slayer](/Mechanics/CLI/items/giant-slayer.md) (DM's choice) for the next 9 days. The charm then vanishes from you and the weapon returns to normal.

## Sources

_Dungeon Master's Guide p. 228_
