_Choke Foes and Enchant Allies_

Magic based on the swirling smoke of strong-smelling herbs has been practiced since the time of ancient elves. Today, the discipline, a mix of aromatherapy and mystical arcana, is kept alive by the so-called "perfumed Witches", who can purify the body and still the mind with their collection of fragrances and herbal smokes. Their Fragrant Magic, however, is not always evident. Sometimes, it would appear their magic is more about relaxation and meditation, while other times, they summon foul clouds that disorientate and kill.

### Fragrant Aura (Level 3)

As a [Magic](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action, you can create a 30-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) of fragrance, which lasts for 1 minute, until you use this feature again to choose a different effect, or you dismiss it (no action required). You choose the type of fragrance from the following options. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). You can also restore your use of it by expending a spell slot (no action required).

**Frankincense.** Each creature you choose in the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) has [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Wisdom checks and saving throws.

**Smelling Salts.** Each [Unconscious](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Unconscious) creature you choose in the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) awakens if it has at least 1 [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). Additionally, each creature in the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) has [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws it makes to avoid or end the [Charmed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Charmed) condition and can't be magically put to sleep.

**Sulfur.** Each creature you choose in the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) has [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on Constitution checks and saving throws.

### Smoke Rings (Level 3)

Whenever you create smoke, such as when you burn herbs or incense or cast [Fog Cloud](Mechanics/CLI/spells/fog-cloud-xphb.md), you can shape it in any manner you choose.

### Aromamancy (Level 6)

You can manipulate your magical fragrances to alter your magic, granting you the following benefits.

**Incense Casting.** When you cast a Witch spell, you can burn a stick of incense to cast it without spell components. A Witch spell you cast in this way can't be affected by [Counterspell](Mechanics/CLI/spells/counterspell-xphb.md). If the spell requires Material components that have a cost specified in the spell, you must burn incense of equal value. A block of incense is worth 2 GP and contains 10 sticks of incense, each worth 2 SP.

**Perfumed Charisma.** You can expend a spell slot as a [Magic](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action to create and apply a magical perfume to yourself. For 1 hour, you have [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on all Charisma checks made to influence a creature within 5 feet of yourself.

### Dizzying Fog (Level 10)

As a [Magic](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action, you can release a cloud of multi-hued smoke in a 20-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md). Each creature you choose within the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC or have the [Poisoned](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, a [Poisoned](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature repeats the save, ending the effect on itself on a success.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

### Olfactory Aura (Level 14)

You are constantly surrounded by a flowery fog in a 30-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md). Whenever an [Ally](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/ally-xphb.md) within the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) makes a [D20 Test](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md), it can add a `d4` to the roll.
