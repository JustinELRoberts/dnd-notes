*Build and Command Clockwork Minions*

Masters of machinery, the tinkers and gearsmiths of the Clockworkers' Guild study and perfect the art of fashioning Constructs. Their designs are as elegant as they are functional, translating single ticking movements through a kaleidoscope of gears and pistons to create lifelike, even a seemingly intelligent, clockwork creatures. Nearly all Clockworkers are attended by a pair of their Construct servants that serve a dual purpose as attentive assistants and relentless bodyguards. Veteran Clockworkers, however, walk astride Constructs taller than themselves, great gear-driven golems of formidable strength.

## Subclass Features

### Clockwork Constructs (Level 3)

You assemble two mechanical servants, which use the [Clockwork Construct](Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/construct/clockwork-construct-valdaspire24.md) stat block. Choose the design for each Construct: a Bolter or Macer. You also determine their appearance, such as if they resemble tin soldiers or mechanical Beasts.

The Constructs are [Friendly](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md) to you and your allies and obey your commands.

**Constructs in Combat.** In combat, the Constructs share your initiative count, but take their turns immediately before or after yours (your choice). Each Construct can move and use its [Reaction](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) on its own.

You can take a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to command one or both Constructs to take an action on its stat block or some other action on its turn. If it receives no command, a Construct can only take the [Dodge](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Dodge) action on its turn. If you have the [Incapacitated](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition, the Constructs take the [Dodge](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Dodge) action and move only to avoid harm.

**Repairing the Constructs.** Your Constructs gain the benefits of taking a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) as normal. You can replace Constructs that have been lost when you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). You can also change the design of each Construct when you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

Customizing your Constructs. Each of your Constructs comes with an integrated weapon. When you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md), you can replace these weapons with Simple or Martial weapons of your choice. A Bolter can only wield Ranged weapons with [Ammunition](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Ammunition), whereas a Macer can only wield Melee weapons that lack [Heavy](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Heavy). If you integrate a Masterwork weapon into a Construct, it gains the benefits of your Masterwork Bonus.

### Mechanical Arm (Level 3)

When you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md), you can attach a mechanized arm to any suit of Masterwork armor. This arm follows your telepathic commands and can be used to hold weapons and [Shields](Mechanics/CLI/items/shield-xphb.md) (allowing you to simultaneously hold a [Two-Handed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Two-Handed) weapon and a one-handed weapon), perform Somatic components of spells, and perform other actions, though the arm doesn't grant you additional actions. If another creature wears the armor, the mechanical arm doesn't function.

### Mechanized Attachments (Level 6)

When you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md), you can use your Crafting Tools and materials worth 25+ GP to affix one of the following attachments to a suit of armor or one of your Clockwork Constructs, or to replace an attachment with another one. Each armor or Construct can have one attachment at a time.

**Clockwork Lantern.** A Bullseye Lantern fixed to your armor casts [Bright Light](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 60-foot [Cone](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cone-area-of-effect-xphb.md) and [Dim Light](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 60 feet. The [Cone](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cone-area-of-effect-xphb.md) of light follows wherever you look. You can light or extinguish the lantern as a [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action.

**Mechanized Grappling Hook.** This launcher contains 80 feet of rope affixed to a [Grappling](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/grappling-xphb.md) Hook. As a [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action, you can launch the [Grappling](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/grappling-xphb.md) Hook at a railing, ledge, or another catch within 80 feet of yourself, the hook catches on if you succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity ([Acrobatics](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Acrobatics)) check. A mechanized winch then retracts the rope and up to 350 pounds of attached weight at a rate of 10 feet per round (100 feet per minute).

**Ram Helm.** A pneumatic Portable Ram is integrated into this armor's helmet, which can be used without hands.

**Springheels.** While wearing this armor, your jump distance is doubled.

### Death Burst (Level 10)

A dead man's switch is integrated into each of your constructs. When one of your Clockwork Constructs drops to 0 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), it detonates in a 5-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md). Each creature within the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) makes a Dexterity saving throw (DC 8 plus your Intelligence modifier and [Proficiency Bonus](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md)), taking `6d6` Fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a failed save.

### Construct Enchantments (Level 14)

You can place a Weapon Enchantment on each of your Clockwork Constructs' integrated weapons. Your Constructs gain the benefits of this enchantment and have [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on attack rolls.
