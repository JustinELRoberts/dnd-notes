*Cook Monsters into Culinary Masterpieces*

Gourmands travel the land in search of the perfect flavor. To this end, they scrounge up rare ingredients from far-off continents, hunt monsters for unique proteins, and tinker with their recipes wherever they travel.

## Subclass Features

### Fine Cuisine (Level 3)

Your culinary experience grants you the following benefits.

**Cook's Utensils.** You gain proficiency with [Cook's Utensils](Mechanics/CLI/items/cooks-utensils-xphb.md) if you don't already have it.

**Gentle Repose.** You can cast [Gentle Repose](Mechanics/CLI/spells/gentle-repose-xphb.md) but only as a [Ritual](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/ritual-xphb.md). Wisdom is yourspellcasting ability for it.

### Monster Meal (Level 3)

As part of a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md), you can prepare and eat a special meal if you have ingredients and [Cook's Utensils](Mechanics/CLI/items/cooks-utensils-xphb.md) on hand. You can prepare enough food for a number of creatures equal to half your Vagabond level plus your Constitution modifier. A creature that eats the food gains [Temporary Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) equal to one roll of your Battle Die plus your Constitution modifier without expending it.

If you add ingredients taken from a monster, a creature that eats the meal also gains a related benefit as shown on the Monster Meal table until it finishes a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). You can add up to three monster ingredients to a single meal and gain the benefits of each. Generally, the amount of ingredients you can harvest from a single monster's corpse is sufficient for one meal.

### Carve [Maneuver] (Level 6)

When you hit a creature with an attack using a weapon or [Unarmed Strike](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md), you can expend two Battle Dice as a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to attempt to butcher the target. Add the Battle Dice to the attack's damage roll. If the creature's type is listed on the Monster Meal table, you gain the related benefit for 1 hour.

### Quick Snack (Level 10)

At the start of each of your turns, you can eat a snack to use your Breather (no action required). Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

You can also restore your use of it by expending one Battle Die (no action required).

### Gourmet Ingredients (Level 14)

When you prepare your Monster Meal, you can choose one of the following benefits for each creature that eats the meal until it finishes a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

**Hero Sauce.** The creature gains [Heroic Inspiration](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md). It can have this instance of [Heroic Inspiration](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md) and another instance at one time.

**Munchkin Spice.** The creature chooses one of its ability scores, which increases by 2, to a maximum of 22.

**Wish Bone.** The creature can cast one spell of level 5 or lower that has a casting time of an action without a spell slot.
