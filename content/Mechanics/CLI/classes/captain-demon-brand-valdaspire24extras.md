_Harness the Demon Within You_

Captains bearing an abyssal Demon Brand have struck the darkest of pacts: binding their soul to a demonic entity that periodically takes hold of their body. On the battlefield, they forgo the companionship of a Cohort and rely on a flickering symbiosis between themselves and their demon. The demon and Captain are two halves of the same creature, sharing a single body and parts of one soul.

## Subclass Features

### Demon Cohort (Level 3)

A demonic entity takes up residence within your body. The demon is jealously possessive—it becomes your Cohort and you can't gain a new one. Unlike other Cohorts, the demon isn't a separate entity and doesn't take its own turn. Instead, you transform into the demon using the following rules.

**Shape-Shift.** You can use a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to shape-shift into the demon or return to your normal form. While shape-shifted, your game statistics are replaced by the [Demon Cohort's](Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fiend/demon-cohort-valdaspire24.md) stat block, but you retain your [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), [Hit Point Dice](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md), Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores, class features, and feats. You choose whether your equipment merges into the demon or is worn by it.

**Temporary Hit Points.** When you are shape-shifted into the demon, you have [Temporary Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the demon's [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), which last until you return to your normal form. When you lose [Temporary Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md), the demon loses an equal number of [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). If you no longer have [Temporary Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) or the demon is reduced to 0 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), you return to your normal form and can't shape-shift into the demon until the demon regains any [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md).

**Demon in Combat.** Unlike other Cohorts, the demon doesn't take a separate turn in combat.

**Restoring the Demon.** The demon gains the benefits of taking a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) as normal. Spells and other effects that restore [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) can target you or the demon while you are shape-shifted. When the demon regains [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), the number of [Temporary Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) it grants you increases by an equal number. If the demon is reduced to 0 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), it regains 1 [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) after 1 minute, allowing you to shape-shift into it again.

### Abyssal Arcana (Level 3)

Your demonic connection grants you the ability to expend Battle Dice to cast the following spells without a spell slot. When you cast a spell using this feature, its duration is 1 minute if it were normally longer.

**1 Battle Die.** [Detect Magic](Mechanics/CLI/spells/detect-magic-xphb.md), [Disguise Self](Mechanics/CLI/spells/disguise-self-xphb.md), [Expeditious Retreat](Mechanics/CLI/spells/expeditious-retreat-xphb.md), [Silent Image](Mechanics/CLI/spells/silent-image-xphb.md)

**2 Battle Dice.** [Alter Self](Mechanics/CLI/spells/alter-self-xphb.md), [Darkness](Mechanics/CLI/spells/darkness-xphb.md), [Darkvision](Mechanics/CLI/spells/darkvision-xphb.md), [Detect Thoughts](Mechanics/CLI/spells/detect-thoughts-xphb.md)

**3 Battle Dice.** [Dispel Magic](Mechanics/CLI/spells/dispel-magic-xphb.md), [Nondetection](Mechanics/CLI/spells/nondetection-xphb.md), [Tongues](Mechanics/CLI/spells/tongues-xphb.md)

### Blindsight (Level 6)

You have [Blindsight](Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Blindsight) with a range of 30 feet while shape-shifted and in your normal form.

### Instinctive Transformation (Level 10)

When you take damage, you can take a [Reaction](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to shape-shift into the demon or return to your normal form. You have [Resistance](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to the triggering damage.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) unless you expend one Battle Die (no action required) to restore your use of it.

### Abyssal Regeneration (Level 14)

At the start of each of your turns, the demon regains [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to 5 plus your Charisma modifier if it is [Bloodied](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Bloodied).
