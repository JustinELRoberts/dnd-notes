_Cripple Your Foes with Deadly Poisons_

For every Alchemist that devotes their life to healing the sick, another hears a darker calling. Commonly called poisoners or Venomsmiths, these Alchemists are masters in the art of crafting deadly and debilitating poisons. Whether employed by an assassin's guild or performing assassinations themselves, a Venomsmith's presence is usually accompanied by untimely death.

## Subclass Features

### Poisoner (Level 3)

You gain the [Alchemy of Poison](/Mechanics/CLI/optional-features/alchemy-of-poison-valdaspire24.md) discovery and can ignore the prerequisite of Alchemist level 5 for brewing poisons. This discovery doesn't count against your number of discoveries known, and you can't later replace this discovery with a different one.

### Laughing Gas Bombs (Level 3)

You gain the [Laughing Gas Bomb](/Mechanics/CLI/optional-features/laughing-gas-bomb-valdaspire24.md) formula, which doesn't count against your total number of formulae known. If you already have it, gain another formula of your choice. For you, the [Bomb](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) is improved in the following ways.

**Improved Damage.** All of this [Bomb's](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) damage dice are d10s.

**Side-Splitter.** A creature that rolls a 1 on the `d20` for its saving throw against the Explode property of this [Bomb](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) has the [Prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) condition and its [Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is 0 until the start of your next turn.

### Alchemical Assassin (Level 6)

You have a specially-designed needle ring for poisoning others. You can use a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to deal 1 Piercing damage to a creature within 5 feet of you, subjecting the creature to contact and injury poisons on the ring. You have [Advantage](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Dexterity ([Sleight of Hand](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Sleight%20of%20Hand) and [Stealth](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Stealth)) checks made to conceal and use this ring.

### Mithridatism (Level 10)

By administering small doses of poisons and toxins to yourself, you have [Immunity](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/immunity-xphb.md) to Poison damage and the [Poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition.

### Toxic Recompense (Level 14)

Whenever a creature within 5 feet of you hits you with a melee attack, you can take a [Reaction](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to unleash a spray of poisonous vapor. The attacker must succeed on a Constitution saving throw against your Alchemist save DC or have the [Poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 minute. While it has the condition, the creature takes `1d10` Poison damage at the start of each of its turns. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
