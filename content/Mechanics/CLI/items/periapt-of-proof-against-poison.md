# Periapt of Proof against Poison

_Wondrous item, major, rare_

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This delicate silver chain has a brilliant-cut black gem pendant. While you wear it, poisons have no effect on you. You are immune to the [poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition and have immunity to poison damage.
