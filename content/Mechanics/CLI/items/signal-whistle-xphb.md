# Signal Whistle

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 5 cp
- **Weight**: ⏤

When blown as a [Utilize](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Utilize) action, a Signal Whistle produces a sound that can be heard up to 600 feet away.
