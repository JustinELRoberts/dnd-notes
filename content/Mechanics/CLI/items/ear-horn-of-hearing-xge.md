# Ear Horn of Hearing

_Wondrous item, minor, common_

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

While held up to your ear, this horn suppresses the effects of the [deafened](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Deafened) condition on you, allowing you to hear normally.
