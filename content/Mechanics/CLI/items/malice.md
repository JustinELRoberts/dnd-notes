# Malice

_Adventuring gear, poison (inhaled)_

- **Properties**: [Poison](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 250 gp

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become [poisoned](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 1 hour. The [poisoned](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature is [blinded](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Blinded).

_Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 258. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>_
