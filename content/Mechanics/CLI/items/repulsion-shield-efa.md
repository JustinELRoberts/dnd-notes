# Repulsion Shield

_Armor ([shield](/Mechanics/CLI/items/shield.md)), uncommon_

- **Armor Class**: 2
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

You gain a +1 bonus to [Armor Class](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) while wielding this shield.

The shield has 4 charges. While holding it, when a Large or smaller creature within 5 feet of you hits you with a melee attack roll, you can take a [Reaction](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to expend 1 of the shield's charges and push the attacker up to 15 feet straight away. The shield regains `1d4` expended charges daily at dawn.
