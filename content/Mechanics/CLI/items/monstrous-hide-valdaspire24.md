# Monstrous Hide

_Monstrous graft, uncommon_

- **Properties**: [External Slot](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#External%20Slot)
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

_Donor: Any Creature with AC 17+ Without Armor_

Your base [Armor Class](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) equals 13 plus your Dexterity modifier. You can wear armor on top of your Monstrous Hide, and you can use either armor to determine your AC. You can use a Shield and still gain this benefit.
