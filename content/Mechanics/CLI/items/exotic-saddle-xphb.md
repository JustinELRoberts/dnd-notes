# Exotic Saddle

_Tack and harness_

- **Cost**: 60 gp
- **Weight**: 40.0 lbs.

A saddle comes with a bit, a bridle, reins, and any other equipment needed to use the saddle. An Exotic Saddle is required for riding an aquatic or a flying mount.
