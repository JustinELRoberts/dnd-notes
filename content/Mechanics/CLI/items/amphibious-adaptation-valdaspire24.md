# Amphibious Adaptation

_Monstrous graft, uncommon_

- **Properties**: [External Slot](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#External%20Slot)
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

_Donor: Any Creature with the Amphibious or Water Breathing Trait_

This graft attaches the fins and gills of an aquatic creature to your body. You have a [Swim Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/swim-speed-xphb.md) equal to your [Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) and you can breathe air and water.
