# Candle

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 1 cp
- **Weight**: ⏤

For 1 hour, a lit Candle sheds [Bright Light](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 5-foot radius and [Dim Light](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 5 feet.
