# Ballista Trap
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 10 gp

As a [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action, you load this compact, Ballista Trap in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you and aim it in a direction you choose. The trap triggers when a creature enters the area within a 30-foot-long, 5-foot-wide [Line](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/line-area-of-effect-xphb.md) extending from the trap for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there. The Ballista Trap makes a ranged attack roll (bonus equals your Intelligence modifier plus your Masterwork Bonus and [Proficiency Bonus](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md)). On a hit, the target takes Piercing damage equal to `1d8`. A creature can only trigger one Ballista Trap per turn. It takes a [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action to recover the Ballista Trap.

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024)*
