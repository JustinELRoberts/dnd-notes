# Potion of Storm Giant Strength

_Potion, minor, legendary_

When you drink this potion, your Strength score changes to 29 for 1 hour. The potion has no effect on you if your Strength is equal to or greater than that score.

This potion's transparent liquid has floating in it a sliver of fingernail from a [storm giant](Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/giant/storm-giant.md).

_Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 187. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>_
