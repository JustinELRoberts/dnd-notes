# Mess Kit

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 2 sp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This tin box contains a cup and simple cutlery. The box clamps together, and one side can be used as a cooking pan and the other as a plate or shallow bowl.

_Player's Handbook p. 152. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span> and the Basic Rules (2014)_
