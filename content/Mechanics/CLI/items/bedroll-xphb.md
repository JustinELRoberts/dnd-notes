# Bedroll
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 1 gp
- **Weight**: 7.0 lbs.

A Bedroll sleeps one Small or Medium creature. While in a Bedroll, you automatically succeed on saving throws against extreme cold (see the "Dungeon Master's Guide").

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 224. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
