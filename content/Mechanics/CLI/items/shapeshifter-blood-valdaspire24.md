# Shapeshifter Blood
*Monstrous graft, uncommon*  


- **Properties**: [Internal Slot](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Internal%20Slot)

*Donor: Any Creature with the Shape-Shift Action or Bonus Action*

You can shape-shift over the course of 1 minute to perfectly alter your appearance. You decide what you look like, including your height, weight, facial features, sound of your voice, hair length, coloration, and other distinguishing characteristics. You can make yourself appear as a member of another species, though none of your statistics change. You can't appear as a creature of a different size, and your basic shape stays the same.

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024)*
