# Ring of Animal Influence

_Ring, major, rare_\
![](Mechanics/CLI/items/img/ring-of-animal-influence.webp#right)

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

This ring has 3 charges, and it regains `1d3` expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to cast one of the following spells:

- [Animal friendship](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/animal-friendship-xphb.md) (save DC 13)
- [Fear](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/fear-xphb.md) (save DC 13), targeting only beasts that have an Intelligence of 3 or lower
- [Speak with animals](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/speak-with-animals-xphb.md)
