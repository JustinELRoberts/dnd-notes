# Bullseye Lantern

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 10 gp
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

A Bullseye Lantern burns Oil as fuel to cast [Bright Light](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 60-foot Cone and [Dim Light](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 60 feet.
