# Helm of Telepathy

_Wondrous item, major, uncommon (requires attunement)_\
![](Mechanics/CLI/items/img/helm-of-telepathy.webp#right)

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

While wearing this helm, you can use an action to cast the [detect thoughts](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/detect-thoughts-xphb.md) spell (save DC 13) from it. As long as you maintain [concentration](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Concentration) on the spell, you can use a bonus action to send a telepathic message to a creature you are focused on. It can reply—using a bonus action to do so—while your focus on it continues.

While focusing on a creature with [detect thoughts](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/detect-thoughts-xphb.md), you can use an action to cast the [suggestion](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/suggestion-xphb.md) spell (save DC 13) from the helm on that creature. Once used, the suggestion property can't be used again until the next dawn.
