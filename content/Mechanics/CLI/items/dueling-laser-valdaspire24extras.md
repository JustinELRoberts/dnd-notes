# Dueling Laser

_Weapon_

- **Damage**: 2d6 radiant
- **Range**: 30/120
- **Properties**: [Blaster](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Blaster), [Cooldown](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Cooldown), [Futuristic Era](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Futuristic%20Era), [Light](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Light)
- **Cost**: 350 gp
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

A pistol intended for honorable, one-on-one duels, the Dueling Laser fires a blinding streak of light and promptly overheats. Spacers favor a Dueling Laser in their offhand for an infrequent but powerful retort.
