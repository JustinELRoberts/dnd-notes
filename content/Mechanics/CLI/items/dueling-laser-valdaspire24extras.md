# Dueling Laser
*Weapon*  


- **Damage**: 2d6 radiant
- **Range**: 30/120
- **Properties**: [Blaster](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Blaster), [Cooldown](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Cooldown), [Futuristic Era](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Futuristic%20Era), [Light](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Light)

A pistol intended for honorable, one-on-one duels, the Dueling Laser fires a blinding streak of light and promptly overheats. Spacers favor a Dueling Laser in their offhand for an infrequent but powerful retort.
