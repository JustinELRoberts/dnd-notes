# Dwarven Plate

_Heavy armor ([plate armor](Mechanics/CLI/items/plate-armor-xphb.md)), major, very rare_\
![](Mechanics/CLI/items/img/dwarven-plate.webp#right)

- **Armor Class**: 18
- **Strength**: Requires 15 STR.
- **Stealth**: The wearer has disadvantage on Stealth (DEX) checks.
- **Weight**: 65.0 lbs.

While wearing this armor, you gain a +2 bonus to AC. In addition, if an effect moves you against your will along the ground, you can use your reaction to reduce the distance you are moved by up to 10 feet.

_Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 167. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>_
