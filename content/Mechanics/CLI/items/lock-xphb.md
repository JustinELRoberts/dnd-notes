# Lock

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 10 gp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

A Lock comes with a key. Without the key, a creature can use [Thieves' Tools](/Mechanics/CLI/items/thieves-tools-xphb.md) to pick this Lock with a successful DC 15 Dexterity ([Sleight of Hand](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Sleight%20of%20Hand)) check.

## Sources

_Player's Handbook (2024) p. 226. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)_
