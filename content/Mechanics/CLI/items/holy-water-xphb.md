# Holy Water

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 25 gp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

When you take the [Attack](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Attack) action, you can replace one of your attacks with throwing a flask of Holy Water. Target one creature you can see within 20 feet of yourself. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw (DC 8 plus your Dexterity modifier and [Proficiency Bonus](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md)) or take `2d8` Radiant damage if it is a Fiend or an Undead.

_Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 226. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)_
