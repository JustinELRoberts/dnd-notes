# Potion of Invisibility

_Potion, minor, very rare_

This potion's container looks empty but feels as though it holds liquid. When you drink it, you become [invisible](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) for 1 hour. Anything you wear or carry is [invisible](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) with you. The effect ends early if you attack or cast a spell.

_Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 188. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span> and the Basic Rules (2014)_
