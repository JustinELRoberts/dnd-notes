# Goggles of Night

_Wondrous item, minor, uncommon_\
![](Mechanics/CLI/items/img/goggles-of-night.webp#right)

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

While wearing these dark lenses, you have [darkvision](/Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Darkvision) out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have [darkvision](/Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Darkvision), wearing the goggles increases its range by 60 feet.
