# Heavy Legs

_Monstrous graft, uncommon_

- **Properties**: [Legs Slot](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Legs%20Slot)
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

_Donor: Large or Larger Monstrosity_

Once these legs are installed, you can use your [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to kick a creature of Large size or smaller within 5 feet of you. Make an [Unarmed Strike](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md). On a hit, the target takes no damage but is pushed 5 feet away from you.

You also count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity.
