# Oil of Taggit
*Adventuring gear, poison (contact)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 400 gp

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or become [poisoned](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 24 hours. The [poisoned](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature is [unconscious](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Unconscious). The creature wakes up if it takes damage.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 258. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
