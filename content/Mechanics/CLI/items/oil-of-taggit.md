# Oil of Taggit

_Adventuring gear, poison (contact)_

- **Properties**: [Poison](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 400 gp
- **Weight**: ⏤

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or become [poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 24 hours. The [poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature is [unconscious](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Unconscious). The creature wakes up if it takes damage.
