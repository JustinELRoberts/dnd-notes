# Potion of Diminution

_Potion, minor, rare_

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

When you drink this potion, you gain the "reduce" effect of the [enlarge/reduce](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/enlarge-reduce-xphb.md) spell for `1d4` hours (no [concentration](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Concentration) required). The red in the potion's liquid continuously contracts to a tiny bead and then expands to color the clear liquid around it. Shaking the bottle fails to interrupt this process.

_Dungeon Master's Guide p. 187. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>_
