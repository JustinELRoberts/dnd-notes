# Potion of Climbing

_Potion, minor, common_

When you drink this potion, you gain a climbing speed equal to your walking speed for 1 hour. During this time, you have advantage on Strength ([Athletics](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Athletics)) checks you make to climb. The potion is separated into brown, silver, and gray layers resembling bands of stone. Shaking the bottle fails to mix the colors.

_Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 187. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>_
