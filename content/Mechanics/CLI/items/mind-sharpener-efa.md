# Mind Sharpener

_Ring, uncommon (requires attunement)_

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

The item has 4 charges. When you fail a Constitution saving throw to maintain [Concentration](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Concentration), you can take a [Reaction](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) and expend 1 of the item's charges to succeed instead. The item regains `1d4` expended charges daily at dawn.
