# Mind Sharpener

_Ring, uncommon (requires attunement)_

The item has 4 charges. When you fail a Constitution saving throw to maintain [Concentration](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Concentration), you can take a [Reaction](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) and expend 1 of the item's charges to succeed instead. The item regains `1d4` expended charges daily at dawn.

_Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 112_
