# Purple Worm Poison

_Adventuring gear, poison (injury)_

- **Properties**: [Poison](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 2,000 gp
- **Weight**: ⏤

This poison must be harvested from a dead or [incapacitated](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Incapacitated) [purple worm](/Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/monstrosity/purple-worm.md). A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 42 (`12d6`) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
