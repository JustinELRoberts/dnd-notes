# Boots of the Winding Path

_Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)_

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

While wearing these boots, you can take a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to teleport up to 15 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. You must have occupied that space at some point during the current turn.
