# Grenade
*Ammunition*  


- **Cost**: 5 gp
- **Weight**: 0.6 lbs.

Ammunition is required by a weapon that has [Ammunition](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Ammunition). A weapon's description specifies the type of ammunition used by the weapon.
