# Torpor

_Adventuring gear, poison (ingested)_

- **Properties**: [Poison](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 600 gp
- **Weight**: ⏤

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become [poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) for `4d6` hours. The [poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature is [incapacitated](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Incapacitated).
