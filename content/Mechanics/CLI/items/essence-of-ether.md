# Essence of Ether

_Adventuring gear, poison (inhaled)_

- **Properties**: [Poison](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 300 gp
- **Weight**: ⏤

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become [poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 8 hours. The [poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature is [unconscious](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Unconscious). The creature wakes up if it takes damage or if another creature takes an action to shake it awake.
