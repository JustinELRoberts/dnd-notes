# Phaser
*Weapon*  


- **Damage**: 2d4 lightning
- **Range**: 30/120
- **Properties**: [Blaster](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Blaster), [Futuristic Era](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Futuristic%20Era), [Light](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Light)
- **Cost**: 100 gp
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

Phasers aren't designed for effectiveness so much as portability and non-lethality. These blasters are often issued to peace-keepers and emissaries that require personal defense but have no intent to kill.
