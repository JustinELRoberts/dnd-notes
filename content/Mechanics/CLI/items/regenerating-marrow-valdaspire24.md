# Regenerating Marrow

_Monstrous graft, rare_

- **Properties**: [Internal Slot](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Internal%20Slot)

_Donor: Any Creature with the Regeneration Trait_

If you are [Bloodied](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Bloodied) and have at least 1 [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) at the start of each of your turns, you can take a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) and expend one of your Hit Dice, roll the die, and regain a number of [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the roll plus your Constitution modifier (no action required).

_Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024)_
