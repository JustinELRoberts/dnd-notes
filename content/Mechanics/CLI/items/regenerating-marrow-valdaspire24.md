# Regenerating Marrow

_Monstrous graft, rare_

- **Properties**: [Internal Slot](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Internal%20Slot)
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

_Donor: Any Creature with the Regeneration Trait_

If you are [Bloodied](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Bloodied) and have at least 1 [Hit Point](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) at the start of each of your turns, you can take a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) and expend one of your Hit Dice, roll the die, and regain a number of [Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the roll plus your Constitution modifier (no action required).
