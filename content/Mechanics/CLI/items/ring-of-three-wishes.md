# Ring of Three Wishes

_Ring, major, legendary_\
![](Mechanics/CLI/items/img/ring-of-three-wishes.webp#right)

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

While wearing this ring, you can use an action to expend 1 of its 3 charges to cast the [wish](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/wish-xphb.md) spell from it. The ring becomes nonmagical when you use the last charge.
