# Magnifying Glass

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 100 gp
- **Weight**: ⏤

A Magnifying Glass grants [Advantage](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on any ability check made to appraise or inspect a highly detailed item. Lighting a fire with a Magnifying Glass requires light as bright as sunlight to focus, tinder to ignite, and about 5 minutes for the fire to ignite.
