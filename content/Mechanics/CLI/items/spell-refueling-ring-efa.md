# Spell-Refueling Ring

_Ring, uncommon (requires attunement by a spellcaster)_

While wearing this ring, you can recover one expended spell slot as a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md). The recovered slot can be of level 3 or lower. Once used, the ring can't be used again until the next dawn.

_Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 112_
