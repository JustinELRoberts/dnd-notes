# Pole

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 5 cp
- **Weight**: 7.0 lbs.

A Pole is 10 feet long. You can use it to touch something up to 10 feet away. If you must make a Strength ([Athletics](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Athletics)) check as part of a High or [Long Jump](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-jump-xphb.md), you can use the Pole to vault, giving yourself [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on the check.

_Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 228. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)_
