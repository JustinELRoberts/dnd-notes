# Keoghtom's Ointment

_Wondrous item, minor, uncommon_\
![](Mechanics/CLI/items/img/keoghtoms-ointment.webp#right)

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

This glass jar, 3 inches in diameter, contains `1d4 + 1` doses of a thick mixture that smells faintly of aloe. The jar and its contents weigh ½ pound.

As an action, one dose of the ointment can be swallowed or applied to the skin. The creature that receives it regains `2d8 + 2` hit points, ceases to be [poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned), and is cured of any disease.

## Sources

_Dungeon Master's Guide p. 179. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span> (as "Restorative Ointment") and the Basic Rules (2014)_
