# Brazier of Commanding Fire Elementals

_Wondrous item, major, rare_\
![](Mechanics/CLI/items/img/brazier-of-commanding-fire-elementals.webp#right)

- **Weight**: 5.0 lbs.

While a fire burns in this brass brazier, you can use an action to speak the brazier's command word and summon a [fire elemental](Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/elemental/fire-elemental.md), as if you had cast the [conjure elemental](Mechanics/CLI/spells/conjure-elemental-xphb.md) spell. The brazier can't be used this way again until the next dawn.

The brazier weighs 5 pounds.

_Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 156. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>_
