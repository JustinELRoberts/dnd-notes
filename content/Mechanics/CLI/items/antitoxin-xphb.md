# Antitoxin

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 50 gp
- **Weight**: ⏤

As a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can drink a vial of Antitoxin to gain [Advantage](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws to avoid or end the [Poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 hour.
