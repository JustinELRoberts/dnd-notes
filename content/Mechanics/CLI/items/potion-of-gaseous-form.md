# Potion of Gaseous Form
*Potion, minor, rare*  



When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [gaseous form](Mechanics/CLI/spells/gaseous-form-xphb.md) spell for 1 hour (no [concentration](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Concentration) required) or until you end the effect as a bonus action. This potion's container seems to hold fog that moves and pours like water.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 187. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
