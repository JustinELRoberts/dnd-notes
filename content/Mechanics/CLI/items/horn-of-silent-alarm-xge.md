# Horn of Silent Alarm

_Wondrous item, instrument ([horn](/Mechanics/CLI/items/horn.md)), minor, common_

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

This horn has 4 charges. When you use an action to blow it, one creature of your choice can hear the horn's blare, provided the creature is within 600 feet of the horn and not [deafened](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Deafened). No other creature hears sound coming from the horn. The horn regains `1d4` expended charges daily at dawn.

_Xanathar's Guide to Everything p. 137_
