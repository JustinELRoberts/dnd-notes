# Serpent Venom

_Adventuring gear, poison (injury)_

- **Properties**: [Poison](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 200 gp
- **Weight**: ⏤

This poison must be harvested from a dead or [incapacitated](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Incapacitated) [giant poisonous snake](/Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/beast/giant-poisonous-snake.md). A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (`3d6`) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
