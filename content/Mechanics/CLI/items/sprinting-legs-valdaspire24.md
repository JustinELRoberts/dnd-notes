# Sprinting Legs
*Monstrous graft, rare*  


- **Properties**: [Legs Slot](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Legs%20Slot)

*Donor: Any Creature with Speed of 60+ feet*

While this graft is attached, your [Speed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) increases by 10 feet and [Opportunity Attacks](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) have [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) against you.

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024)*
