# Grappling Hook

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 2 gp
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

As a [Utilize](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action, you can throw the Grappling Hook at a railing, a ledge, or another catch within 50 feet of yourself, and the hook catches on if you succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity ([Acrobatics](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Acrobatics)) check. If you tied a Rope to the hook, you can then climb it.

_Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 225. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)_
