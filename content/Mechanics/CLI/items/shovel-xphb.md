# Shovel

_Adventuring gear_

- **Cost**: 2 gp
- **Weight**: 5.0 lbs.

Working for 1 hour, you can use a Shovel to dig a hole that is 5 feet on each side in soil or similar material.

_Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 228. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)_
