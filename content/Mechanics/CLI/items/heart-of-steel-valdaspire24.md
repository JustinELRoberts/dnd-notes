# Heart of Steel

_Monstrous graft, common_

- **Properties**: [Internal Slot](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Internal%20Slot)
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

_Donor: Any Construct_

This Construct core acts as a replacement for one's heart. Once installed, you don't need to sleep, and magic can't put you to sleep. You can finish a [Long Rest](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) in 4 hours if you spend those hours in a motionless state, during which you retain consciousness.
