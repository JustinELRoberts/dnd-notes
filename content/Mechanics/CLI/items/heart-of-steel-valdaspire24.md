# Heart of Steel
*Monstrous graft, common*  


- **Properties**: [Internal Slot](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Internal%20Slot)

*Donor: Any Construct*

This Construct core acts as a replacement for one's heart. Once installed, you don't need to sleep, and magic can't put you to sleep. You can finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) in 4 hours if you spend those hours in a motionless state, during which you retain consciousness.

*Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024)*
