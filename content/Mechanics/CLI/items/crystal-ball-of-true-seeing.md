# Crystal Ball of True Seeing

_Wondrous item, major, legendary (requires attunement)_

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

This [crystal ball](/Mechanics/CLI/items/crystal-ball.md) is about 6 inches in diameter. While touching it, you can cast the [scrying](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/scrying-xphb.md) spell (save DC 17) with it.

While [scrying](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/scrying-xphb.md) with the [crystal ball](/Mechanics/CLI/items/crystal-ball.md), you have [truesight](/Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Truesight) with a radius of 120 feet centered on the spell's sensor.

## Sources

_Dungeon Master's Guide p. 159_
