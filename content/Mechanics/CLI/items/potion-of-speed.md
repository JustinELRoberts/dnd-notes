# Potion of Speed
*Potion, minor, very rare*  



When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [haste](Mechanics/CLI/spells/haste-xphb.md) spell for 1 minute (no [concentration](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Concentration) required). The potion's yellow fluid is streaked with black and swirls on its own.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 188. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
