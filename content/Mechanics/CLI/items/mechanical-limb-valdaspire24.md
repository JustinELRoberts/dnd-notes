# Mechanical Limb

_Monstrous graft, rare_

- **Properties**: [Arms Slot](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Arms%20Slot)

_Donor: Any Medium or Larger Construct_

This additional arm installs beneath your normal arms. This arm can be used to hold weapons and Shields (allowing you to simultaneously hold a two-handed weapon and a one-handed weapon), perform Somatic components of spells, and perform other actions, though the arm doesn't grant you additional actions.

_Source: Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024)_
