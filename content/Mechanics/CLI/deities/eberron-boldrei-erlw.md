# Boldrei
![The Sovereign Host](Mechanics/CLI/deities/img/erlw-the-sovereign-host.webp#symbol)

- **Category**: The Sovereign Host
- **Domains**: Life, Peace, Twilight
- **Pantheon**: Eberron
