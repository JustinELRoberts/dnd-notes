# Tough

Your [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your character level when you gain this feat. Whenever you gain a character level thereafter, your [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) maximum increases by an additional 2 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md).
