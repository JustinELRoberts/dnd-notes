# Boon of Fate

![](Mechanics/CLI/feats/img/boon-of-fate.webp#right)

_**Prerequisites**_ 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Improve Fate.** When you or another creature within 60 feet of you succeeds on or fails a [D20 Test](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md), you can roll `2d4` and apply the total rolled as a bonus or penalty to the `d20` roll. Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you roll [Initiative](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) or finish a [Short](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
