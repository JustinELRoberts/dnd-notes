# Unarmed Fighting

_**Prerequisites**_ Fighting Style Feature

When you hit with your [Unarmed Strike](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md) and deal damage, you can deal Bludgeoning damage equal to `1d6` plus your Strength modifier instead of the normal damage of an [Unarmed Strike](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md). If you aren't holding any weapons or a [Shield](Mechanics/CLI/items/shield-xphb.md) when you make the attack roll, the `d6` becomes a `d8`.

At the start of each of your turns, you can deal `1d4` Bludgeoning damage to one creature [Grappled](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Grappled) by you.
