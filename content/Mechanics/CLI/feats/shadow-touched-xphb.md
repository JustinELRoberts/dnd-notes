# Shadow-Touched 


***Prerequisites*** 4th

Your exposure to the Shadowfell's magic grants you the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Shadow Magic.** Choose one level 1 spell from the Illusion or Necromancy school of magic. You always have that spell and the [Invisibility](Mechanics/CLI/spells/invisibility-xphb.md) spell prepared. You can cast each of these spells without expending a spell slot. Once you cast either spell in this way, you can't cast that spell in this way again until you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). You can also cast these spells using spell slots you have of the appropriate level. The spells' spellcasting ability is the ability increased by this feat.
