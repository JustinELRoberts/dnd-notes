# Crafter

You gain the following benefits.

**Tool Proficiency.** You gain proficiency with three different [Artisan's Tools](Mechanics/CLI/items/artisans-tools-xphb.md) of your choice from the Fast Crafting table.

**Discount.** Whenever you buy a nonmagical item, you receive a 20 percent discount on it.

**Fast Crafting.** When you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md), you can craft one piece of gear from the Fast Crafting table, provided you have the [Artisan's Tools](Mechanics/CLI/items/artisans-tools-xphb.md) associated with that item and have proficiency with those tools. The item lasts until you finish another [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md), at which point the item falls apart.
