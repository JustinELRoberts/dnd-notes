# Wood Elf Magic 


***Prerequisites*** [Elf](Mechanics/CLI/races/elf-xphb.md)

You learn the magic of the primeval woods, which are revered and protected by your people. You learn one druid cantrip of your choice. You also learn the [longstrider](Mechanics/CLI/spells/longstrider-xphb.md) and [pass without trace](Mechanics/CLI/spells/pass-without-trace-xphb.md) spells, each of which you can cast once without expending a spell slot. You regain the ability to cast these two spells in this way when you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for all three spells.
