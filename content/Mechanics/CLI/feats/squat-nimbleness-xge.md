# Squat Nimbleness

_**Prerequisites**_ dwarf or a Small race

You are uncommonly nimble for your race. You gain the following benefits:

- Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- Increase your walking speed by 5 feet.
- You gain proficiency in the [Acrobatics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Acrobatics) or [Athletics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Athletics) skill (your choice).
- You have advantage on any Strength ([Athletics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Athletics)) or Dexterity ([Acrobatics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Acrobatics)) check you make to escape from being [grappled](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Grappled).
