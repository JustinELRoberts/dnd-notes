# Gunner

You have a quick hand and keen eye when employing firearms, granting you the following benefits:

- Increase your Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
- You gain proficiency with firearms (see "[Firearms](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/firearms.md)" in the Dungeon Master's Guide).
- You ignore the [loading](/Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Loading) property of firearms.
- Being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged attack rolls.
