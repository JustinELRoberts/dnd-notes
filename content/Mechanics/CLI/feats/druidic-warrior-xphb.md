# Druidic Warrior

_**Prerequisites**_ When Gaining the Level 2 Ranger "Fighting Style" Feature

You learn two Druid cantrips of your choice. [Guidance](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/guidance-xphb.md) and [Starry Wisp](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/starry-wisp-xphb.md) are recommended. The chosen cantrips count as Ranger spells for you, and Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for them. Whenever you gain a Ranger level, you can replace one of these cantrips with another Druid cantrip.
