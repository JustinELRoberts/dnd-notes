# Greater Mark of Healing 


***Prerequisites*** 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Medical Intuition benefit of your [Mark of Healing](Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-healing-efa.md) feat, you can roll `1d6` instead of `1d4`.

**Improved Healing.** You can now use the Healing Touch benefit of your [Mark of Healing](Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-healing-efa.md) feat to cast [Cure Wounds](Mechanics/CLI/spells/cure-wounds-xphb.md) without using a spell slot a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). Additionally, when you cast [Cure Wounds](Mechanics/CLI/spells/cure-wounds-xphb.md) and roll dice to determine the number of [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) restored, you can treat any 1 or 2 on a roll as a 3.
