# Burning

_Generic Hazard_

A burning creature or object takes `1d4` Fire damage at the start of each of its turns. As an action, you can extinguish fire on yourself by giving yourself the [Prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) condition and rolling on the ground. The fire also goes out if it is doused, submerged, or suffocated.

_Player's Handbook (2024) p. 362_
