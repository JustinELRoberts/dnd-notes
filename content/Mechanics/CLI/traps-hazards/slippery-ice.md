# Slippery Ice

_Wilderness Hazard_

Slippery ice is difficult terrain. When a creature moves onto slippery ice for the first time on a turn, it must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity ([Acrobatics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Acrobatics)) check or fall [prone](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone).

_Dungeon Master's Guide p. 110_
