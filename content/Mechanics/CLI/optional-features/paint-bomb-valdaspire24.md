# Paint Bomb 


You can replace your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) contents with vibrant, magical pigment, changing it into a Paint Bomb. This [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals no damage but instead splatters paint within the [Sphere](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) of the [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) Explode property. A target hit by this [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) or that fails its saving throw against it is coated in paint until the start of your next turn. A coated creature can't benefit from the [Invisible](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) condition and attacks against it have [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md).
