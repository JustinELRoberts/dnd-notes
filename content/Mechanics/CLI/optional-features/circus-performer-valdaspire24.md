# Circus Performer

You learned a few simple tricks to participate in a sideshow or circus act. You learn the [Minor Illusion](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/minor-illusion-xphb.md) and [Starry Wisp](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/starry-wisp-xphb.md) cantrips and you gain proficiency in the [Acrobatics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Acrobatics) skill.
