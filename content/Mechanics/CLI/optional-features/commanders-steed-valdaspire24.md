# Commander's Steed

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 10 Warmage (House of Kings)

You can cast the spell [Find Steed](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/find-steed-xphb.md) without expending a spell slot and regain the ability to do so when you finish a [Long Rest](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). When you cast the spell using this trick, its spell slot level is your Wamage level divided by four, rounded up.
