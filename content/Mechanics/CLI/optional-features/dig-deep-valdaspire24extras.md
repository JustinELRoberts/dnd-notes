# Dig Deep

As a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can expend one Battle Die to gain [Temporary Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the number rolled on the die plus your Constitution modifier.
