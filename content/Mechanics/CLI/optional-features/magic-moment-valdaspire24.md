# Magic Moment

_**Prerequisites**_ [Moment to Think](Mechanics/CLI/spells/moment-to-think-valdaspire24.md) cantrip

When you cast [Moment to Think](Mechanics/CLI/spells/moment-to-think-valdaspire24.md), you can choose to also stop time for a creature you can see within 30 feet of you. The creature can take one of the actions permitted by the spell during your turn, and you can interact with one another while time is stopped. During this time you can take the [Influence](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Influence) action, exchange items, swap locations with a willing creature within 5 feet of you, take the [Help](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Help) action for tasks while time is stopped, attempt to stabilize an [Unconscious](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Unconscious) creature that has 0 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), or have a short conversation without eavesdropping or interruption.
