# Riposte 


***Prerequisites*** Level 11 Vagabond

When you hit a creature with an attack using a weapon or [Unarmed Strike](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md), you can expend three Battle Dice as a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to enter a defensive stance against it. Add the Battle Dice to the attack's damage roll. Once before the start of your next turn, when the target makes an attack against you, you can take a [Reaction](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to make one attack with a weapon against the attacker. Resolve your attack before the triggering attack. On a hit, its attack automatically misses you.
