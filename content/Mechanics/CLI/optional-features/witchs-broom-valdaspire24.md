# Witch's Broom 


You can take a [Magic](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action to enchant a mundane object—such as a broom, cauldron, or rug—with the power of flight. While holding this item, you have a [Fly Speed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/fly-speed-xphb.md) of 60 feet. This object remains enchanted until you use this Grand Hex to enchant a different object.
