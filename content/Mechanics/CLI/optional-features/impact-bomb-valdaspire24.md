# Impact Bomb 


By replacing your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents with a more reactive one, you can change it into an Impact Bomb. This [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals Force damage instead of Fire damage and all of its damage dice are d8s. When you use the [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) Explode property, each creature within the explosion makes a Strength saving throw, instead of Dexterity. A Huge or smaller target hit by this [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) or that fails its saving throw against it has the [Prone](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition.
