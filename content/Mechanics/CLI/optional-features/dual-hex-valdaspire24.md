# Dual Hex 


When you cast a Hex as a [Magic](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action, you can cast a second Hex or cast the original Hex a second time targeting a different creature as a part of the same action. Both Hexes must have a casting time of an action. When you [Cackle](Mechanics/CLI/classes/witch-valdaspire24.md#Cackle%20(Level%202)), you extend the duration of both Hexes by 1 round.

Each target affected by your Hexes counts as being the sole target of your Hex for [Insidious Spell](Mechanics/CLI/classes/witch-valdaspire24.md#Insidious%20Spell%20(Level%205)) and other Witch features.
