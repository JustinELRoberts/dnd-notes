# Possessed 


Your soul is occupied by a foreign spirit that whispers magical secrets to you and sometimes tries to wrest away your consciousness. You can prepare an extra Witch spell. You can prepare another extra Witch spell when you reach Witch levels 3 (2 spells) and 5 (3 spells).
