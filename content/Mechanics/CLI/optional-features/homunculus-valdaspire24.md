# Homunculus

Your research has taught you how to create an alchemical familiar: a Homunculus.

## Creating a Homunculus

By expending 3 [Reagents](/Mechanics/CLI/classes/alchemist-valdaspire24.md#Reagents%20\(Level%201\)) and spending materials worth 10+ GP in a 1-hour long alchemical process, which can be done over a [Short Rest](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md), you can create a homunculus. You can only have one homunculus at a time; if you create another one, your existing one dies.

## Hit Points

Your homunculus has [Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to five times your Alchemist level.

## Bomb Immunity

Your homunculus automatically succeeds on its saving throws against your Bombs and never takes damage from them.

## Combat

The homunculus is an ally to you and your allies. It takes its turn immediately before or after your turn each round (your choice). Your homunculus can't attack on its turn, but it can take other actions and [Bonus Actions](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) as normal. Additionally, you can take a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to allow your homunculus to make one attack with its [Reaction](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md). Your homunculus's attack bonus equals your Intelligence modifier plus your [Proficiency Bonus](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md).

## Elemental Infusion

As a [Magic](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Magic) action, you can expend 2 [Reagents](/Mechanics/CLI/classes/alchemist-valdaspire24.md#Reagents%20\(Level%201\)) to infuse your homunculus with elemental essence for the next hour. Choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder damage. While infused, your homunculus has [Resistance](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to this damage and deals `2d8` extra damage of that type on its damage rolls.
