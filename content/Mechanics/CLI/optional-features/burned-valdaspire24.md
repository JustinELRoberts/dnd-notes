# Burned 


Your body has been scorched by arcane flames, leaving you with striking scars and embers burning under your skin. You have [Resistance](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Fire damage, and you know the [Produce Flame](Mechanics/CLI/spells/produce-flame-xphb.md) cantrip, which doesn't count against your number of cantrips known.
