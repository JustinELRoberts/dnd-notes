# Bishop's Maneuver

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 10 Warmage (House of Bishops)

As a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can expend spell slot to teleport to an unoccupied space you can see. The number of feet you can teleport equals 15 feet times the level of the spell slot expended.

## Sources

_Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024)_
