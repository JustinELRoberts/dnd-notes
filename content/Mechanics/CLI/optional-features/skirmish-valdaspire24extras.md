# Skirmish

When you make an attack using a weapon, you can expend one Battle Die as a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to make an attack using a weapon later on the same turn. One attack must be a melee attack roll and the other must be a ranged attack roll.
