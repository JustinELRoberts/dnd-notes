# Nobility

You are of noble descent, now disgraced or disowned. You gain proficiency in the [History](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#History) and [Persuasion](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Persuasion) skills.

Additionally, you can take the [Influence](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Influence) action as a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md).
