# Dying

A terminal, possibly magical, affliction has gripped you. When you roll [Initiative](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) and aren't [Bloodied](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Bloodied), you can drop to a number of [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to half your maximum and become [Bloodied](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Bloodied). Additionally, you have [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on [Death Saving Throws](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/death-saving-throw-xphb.md).
