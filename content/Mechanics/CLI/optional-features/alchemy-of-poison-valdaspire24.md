# Alchemy of Poison 


Your research has taught you how to concoct poisonous compounds. You gain the following benefits.

## Batch Brewing

The number of potions you can have at one time increases by 2.

## Poisoner's Kit

You gain proficiency in the [Poisoner's Kit](Mechanics/CLI/items/poisoners-kit-xphb.md).

## Potent Poison

When you make a damage roll that deals Poison damage, it ignores [Resistance](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Poison damage.

## Poison Crafting

You can brew the following poisons at the Alchemist levels given. If a poisons forces a creature to make a saving throw, it uses your Alchemist save DC. These poisons are included in the 5th Edition rules.
