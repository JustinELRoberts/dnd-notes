# Contingent Cantrip 


When you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md), you can spend 10 minutes performing a ritual to create a Contingent [Cantrip](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cantrip-xphb.md). Cast a Warmage cantrip that has a casting time of an action or [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) and doesn't deal damage. The cantrip doesn't take effect immediately, but instead it takes effect the next time you speak a specified command word (no action required). When you perform this ritual, choose whether the cantrip targets you, the nearest [Ally](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/ally-xphb.md), or the nearest [Enemy](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/enemy-xphb.md). You can't use Warmage tricks on the cantrip when it comes into effect. You can only have one Contingent [Cantrip](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cantrip-xphb.md) at a time. If you perform this ritual again, the previous Contingent [Cantrip](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cantrip-xphb.md) ends.
