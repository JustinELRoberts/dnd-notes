# Blinding Cantrip

_**Prerequisites**_ [Light](Mechanics/CLI/spells/light-xphb.md) cantrip

When you cast the [Light](Mechanics/CLI/spells/light-xphb.md) cantrip on an object you are holding, you can direct a flare at a creature you can see within 10 feet of yourself, which must make a Constitution saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failed save, the target has the [Blinded](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Blinded) condition until the start of your next turn. A creature that fails its save against this effect is immune to it for 1 minute, after which it can be affected again.
