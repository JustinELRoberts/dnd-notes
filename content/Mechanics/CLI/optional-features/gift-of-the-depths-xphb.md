# Gift of the Depths

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 5

You can breathe underwater, and you gain a [Swim Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/swim-speed-xphb.md) equal to your [Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md).

You can also cast [Water Breathing](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/water-breathing-xphb.md) once without expending a spell slot. You regain the ability to cast it in this way again when you finish a [Long Rest](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

## Sources

_Player's Handbook (2024) p. 156_
