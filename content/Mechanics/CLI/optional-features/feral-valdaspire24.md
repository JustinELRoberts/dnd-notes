Through your curse, you have forgotten the manners and customs of civilization. You have proficiency in the [Survival](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Survival) skill. Additionally, while you aren't wearing armor, your base [Armor Class](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) equals 13 plus your Dexterity modifier.
