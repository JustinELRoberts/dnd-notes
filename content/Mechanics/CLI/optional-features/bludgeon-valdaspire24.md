# Bludgeon 


***Prerequisites*** Level 2 Craftsman, Ranged Weapon

You can treat this weapon as a Melee weapon with [Finesse](Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Finesse). When you hit a creature with a melee attack using this weapon, it deals Bludgeoning damage equal to `1d6` plus your Strength or Dexterity modifier, or `1d8` plus your Strength or Dexterity modifier if the weapon is wielded in two hands.
