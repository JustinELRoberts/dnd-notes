# Infested 


You are constantly followed by vermin, like insects and rats, which crawl on your skin and swarm in your wake. Beasts have [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on attacks against you. Additionally, you can command these pests as your own. At Witch level 2, you can choose the form of a Swarm of Insects or a Swarm of Rats for your familiar.
