# Silent Cantrip 


Once on each of your turns immediately after you deal Thunder damage to an enemy with a Warmage cantrip, you can create a 5-foot [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) of magical silence originating from the target, which lasts until the start of your next turn. Any creature or object entirely inside the [Emanation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) has [Immunity](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/immunity-xphb.md) to Thunder damage, and creatures have the [Deafened](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Deafened) condition while entirely inside it. Casting a spell that includes a Verbal component is impossible there.
