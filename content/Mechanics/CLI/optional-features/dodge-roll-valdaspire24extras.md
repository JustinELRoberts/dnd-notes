# Dodge Roll

You can expend one Battle Die as a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to move up to 15 feet and reload any Ranged weapon you are holding. This movement doesn't provoke [Opportunity Attacks](/Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) and is unaffected by [Difficult Terrain](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/difficult-terrain-xphb.md).
