# Follow-Through 


***Prerequisites*** Level 5 Craftsman, Masterwork Melee Weapon, Heavy Property

When you take the [Attack](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Attack) action and hit a creature with a melee attack using this weapon, you can replace one of your attacks with a heavier swing. You deal `2d12` extra damage to that creature of the same type dealt by the weapon. You can deal this extra damage only once per turn.
