# Necrobiology

Your explorations into necromancy have borne scientific fruit. You gain the following benefits.

## Hemoreagent

You can take a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to extract a measure of your own blood and convert it into [Reagents](Mechanics/CLI/classes/alchemist-valdaspire24.md#Reagents%20\(Level%201\)). Expend any number of [Hit Point Dice](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md). You regain a number of expended [Reagents](Mechanics/CLI/classes/alchemist-valdaspire24.md#Reagents%20\(Level%201\)) equal to half the number of [Hit Point Dice](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md) expended (round down).

## Lazarus Bolt

As a [Magic](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action, you can drive a bolt into the torso of a creature within 5 feet of you that has died within the last minute and shock it back to life. That creature revives with 1 [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) and gains [Temporary Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) equal to twice your Alchemist level. This discovery can't return to life a creature that lacks a heart, a creature that has died of old age, or a creature that is missing vital organs. Once you use this ability, you can't use it again until you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). You can also restore your use of it by expending 5 [Reagents](Mechanics/CLI/classes/alchemist-valdaspire24.md#Reagents%20\(Level%201\)) (no action required).
