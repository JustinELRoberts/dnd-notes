# Quiet Bomb 


By replacing your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents with a pouch of rubber pellets, you can change it into a Quiet Bomb. This [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals Bludgeoning damage instead of Fire damage. When this [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) detonates, it makes only a low thud, audible out to 10 feet, and releases a puff of smoke to mask the light of the blast. Additionally, when you reduce a creature to 0 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) with this [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md), you can instead knock it out.

The creature is instead reduced to 1 [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). The creature remains [Unconscious](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Unconscious) until it regains any [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) or until someone uses an action to administer first aid to it, which requires a successful DC 10 Wisdom ([Medicine](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Medicine)) check.
