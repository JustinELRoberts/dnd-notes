# Feinting Attack

As a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can expend one Superiority Die to feint, choosing one creature within 5 feet of yourself as your target. You have [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on your next attack roll against that target this turn. If that attack hits, add the Superiority Die to the attack's damage roll.

_Player's Handbook (2024) p. 95_
