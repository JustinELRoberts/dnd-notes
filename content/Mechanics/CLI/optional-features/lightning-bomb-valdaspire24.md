# Lightning Bomb 


By replacing your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents with a charged arcane capacitor, you can change it into a Lightning Bomb. This [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals Lightning damage instead of Fire damage. A target hit by this [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) or that fails its saving throw against it can't make [Opportunity Attacks](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) until the start of its next turn.
