# Prismatic Cantrip 


Once on each of your turns, when you deal Force damage with a Warmage cantrip, you can roll a `d6` on the Prismatic Cantrip table to change the cantrip's damage type for this casting. You can apply tricks to the casting as normal using the new damage type. Additionally, you can apply the trick corresponding to the rolled damage type, even if you don't know the trick.

You can use this trick a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

**Prismatic Cantrip**

| dice: d6 | Damage Type | Warmage Trick |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| 1 | Acid | [Corrosive Cantrip](Mechanics/CLI/optional-features/corrosive-cantrip-valdaspire24.md) |
| 2 | Cold | [Icy Cantrip](Mechanics/CLI/optional-features/icy-cantrip-valdaspire24.md) |
| 3 | Fire | [Explosive Cantrip](Mechanics/CLI/optional-features/explosive-cantrip-valdaspire24.md) |
| 4 | Lightning | [Static Cantrip](Mechanics/CLI/optional-features/static-cantrip-valdaspire24.md) |
| 5 | Poison | [Draining Cantrip](Mechanics/CLI/optional-features/draining-cantrip-valdaspire24.md) |
| 6 | Thunder | [Silent Cantrip](Mechanics/CLI/optional-features/silent-cantrip-valdaspire24.md) |
