# Mounted

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 2 Craftsman, Ranged Weapon, Heavy Property

You can take a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to mount this weapon in a fixed position until the end of your turn. While mounted, this weapon can't be moved and its damage dice increase by one step (`d4` → `d6` → `d8` → `d10` → `d12`, to a maximum of d12s) when the weapon is used to make a ranged attack.
