# Poison Apple 


As a [Magic](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action, you can produce a magical apple out of thin air. You determine the apple's appearance, such as a ruby-red color or a crystalline skin. The apple shrivels and becomes nonmagical if it isn't eaten within 24 hours. Once you produce an apple, you can't produce another one until you finish a [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

## Eating the Apple

You can take a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to eat the apple, granting the following benefits.

**Hit Points.** You regain [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to twice your Witch level plus your Charisma modifier.  
**Regain a Spell Slot.** You regain an expended spell slot of level 5 or lower.  
**End Condition.** You can remove one of the following conditions from yourself: [Blinded](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Deafened), [Paralyzed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Paralyzed) (if the apple is fed to you as an action), or [Poisoned](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned).  

## Another Creature Eating the Apple

If a creature other than yourself eats the apple, it doesn't gain any benefits. Instead, it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC with [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) or suffer the following effects.

**Poisoned.**The creature has the [Poisoned](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 24 hours.  
**Hit Point Loss.** At the end of each hour while the creature has the [Poisoned](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition, it takes `4d8` Necrotic damage and its [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the Necrotic damage taken. This damage ignores [Resistance](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) and [Immunity](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/immunity-xphb.md). If this reduction causes the creature to drop to 0 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), the creature instead drops to 1 [Hit Point](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) and has the [Unconscious](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Unconscious) condition, falling into a catatonic sleep for 1 year. A [Remove Curse](Mechanics/CLI/spells/remove-curse-xphb.md) spell or similar magic can awaken this creature early.
