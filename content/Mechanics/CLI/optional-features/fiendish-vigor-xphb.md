# Fiendish Vigor

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 2

You can cast [False Life](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/false-life-xphb.md) on yourself without expending a spell slot. When you cast the spell with this feature, you don't roll the die for the [Temporary Hit Points](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md); you automatically get the highest number on the die.

_Player's Handbook (2024) p. 155. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span>_
