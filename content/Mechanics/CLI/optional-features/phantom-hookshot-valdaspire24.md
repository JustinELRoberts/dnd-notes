# Phantom Hookshot 


***Prerequisites*** [Phantom Grapnel](Mechanics/CLI/spells/phantom-grapnel-valdaspire24.md) cantrip

You can cast the [Phantom Grapnel](Mechanics/CLI/spells/phantom-grapnel-valdaspire24.md) cantrip as a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md). If you do so, its range is reduced to 15 feet. When you cast the spell as an action, the target is pulled an additional 10 feet toward you by the spell.
