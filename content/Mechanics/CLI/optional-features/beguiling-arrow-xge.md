# Beguiling Arrow

Your enchantment magic causes this arrow to temporarily beguile its target. The creature hit by the arrow takes an extra `2d6` psychic damage, and choose one of your allies within 30 feet of the target. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw, or it is [charmed](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Charmed) by the chosen ally until the start of your next turn. This effect ends early if the chosen ally attacks the [charmed](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Charmed) target, deals damage to it, or forces it to make a saving throw.

The psychic damage increases to `4d6` when you reach 18th level in this class.
