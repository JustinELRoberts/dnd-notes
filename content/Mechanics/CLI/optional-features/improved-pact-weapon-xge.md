# Improved Pact Weapon

_**Prerequisites**_ Pact of the Blade

You can use any weapon you summon with your Pact of the Blade feature as a spellcasting focus for your warlock spells.

In addition, the weapon gains a +1 bonus to its attack and damage rolls, unless it is a magic weapon that already has a bonus to those rolls.

Finally, the weapon you conjure can be a [shortbow](Mechanics/CLI/items/shortbow-xphb.md), [longbow](Mechanics/CLI/items/longbow-xphb.md), [light crossbow](Mechanics/CLI/items/light-crossbow-xphb.md), or [heavy crossbow](Mechanics/CLI/items/heavy-crossbow-xphb.md).
