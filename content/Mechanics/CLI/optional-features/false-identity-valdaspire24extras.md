# False Identity 


You magically conceal your true appearance. You can cast [Disguise Self](Mechanics/CLI/spells/disguise-self-xphb.md) without a spell slot or components. When you cast the spell in this way, you can only change your appearance to match a pre-established second identity. Whenever you gain a Vagabond level, you can change how this second identity looks when you cast the spell in this way.

Additionally, when you choose this secret, choose one falsehood about yourself can recite it perfectly. This falsehood can be a single lie or an entire false history. You never need to make a [Deception](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Deception) check for this falsehood.
