# Laughing Gas Bomb

By mixing your [Bomb's](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents with a drop of jesterate, a highly euphoric chemical, you can change it into a Laughing Gas Bomb. This [Bomb](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals Poison damage instead of Fire damage and all of its damage dice are d8s. When you use the [Bomb's](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) Explode property, each creature within the explosion makes a Constitution saving throw, instead of Dexterity. A target hit by this [Bomb](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) or that fails its saving throw against it laughs uncontrollably and has the [Poisoned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition and can't cast spells with a Verbal component until the start of your next turn.
