# Flexible Range 


Being within 5 feet of an enemy doesn't impose [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on your ranged attack rolls with Warmage cantrips. Additionally, when you cast a Warmage cantrip that requires a melee spell attack, you can increase its range to 10 feet.
