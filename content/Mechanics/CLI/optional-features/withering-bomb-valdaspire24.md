# Withering Bomb 


By mixing your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents with a powerful desiccating agent, you can change it into a Withering Bomb. This [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals Necrotic damage instead of Fire damage and all of its damage dice are d8s. When you use the [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) Explode property, each creature within the explosion makes a Constitution saving throw, instead of Dexterity. A target hit by this [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) or that fails its saving throw against it has a -3 penalty on saving throws it makes before the start of your next turn.
