# Bramble Bomb

By adding a soil and a potent fertilizer to your [Bomb's](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents, you can change it into a Bramble Bomb. This [Bomb](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals no damage. When you use the [Bomb's](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) Explode property, each creature within the explosion makes a Strength saving throw, instead of Dexterity. The area of the explosion is filled with thorny brambles and is [Difficult Terrain](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/difficult-terrain-xphb.md). A target on the ground hit by this [Bomb](/Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) or that fails its saving throw against it is entangled in the brambles and its [Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is 0 and can't increase. An entangled creature can take an action to make a Strength ([Athletics](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Athletics)) check against your Alchemist save DC. On a success, it frees itself from the brambles and its [Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is no longer 0. The brambles shrivel and die after 1 minute.
