# Nudge Fate 


***Prerequisites*** Level 5 Warmage, [Cheat](Mechanics/CLI/spells/cheat-valdaspire24.md) cantrip

You can cast [Cheat](Mechanics/CLI/spells/cheat-valdaspire24.md) without Verbal or Somatic components.

Additionally, when you fail a [D20 Test](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md), you can nudge the `d20`, changing the number to one of the three sides adjacent to the topmost side, potentially causing the roll to succeed. Alternatively, you can reroll the die and must use the new result.

Once you use this trick to nudge a `d20`, you can't use it to do so again until you finish a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
