# Temple Initiate 


A monastery or temple educated you in the ways of gentle healing magic. You learn the [Sacred Flame](Mechanics/CLI/spells/sacred-flame-xphb.md) and [Spare the Dying](Mechanics/CLI/spells/spare-the-dying-xphb.md) cantrips and you gain proficiency in the [Religion](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Religion) skill.
