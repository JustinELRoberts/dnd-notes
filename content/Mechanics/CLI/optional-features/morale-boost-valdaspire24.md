# Morale Boost

When an [Ally](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/ally-xphb.md) you can see within 60 feet of yourself fails a saving throw, you can take a [Reaction](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to expend one Battle Die and add it to the roll, potentially turning it into a success.
