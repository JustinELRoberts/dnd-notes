# Commanding Presence

When you make a Charisma ([Intimidation](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Intimidation), [Performance](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Performance), or [Persuasion](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Persuasion)) check, you can expend one Superiority Die and add that die to the roll.

## Sources

_Player's Handbook (2024) p. 95_
