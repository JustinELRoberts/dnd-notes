# Extended Range

When you cast a Warmage cantrip that has a range of 5 feet or greater, you can double its range. You can also double the length of [Lines](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/line-area-of-effect-xphb.md), the size of [Cones](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/cone-area-of-effect-xphb.md) and [Cubes](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/cube-area-of-effect-xphb.md), and the radii of [Cylinders](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/cylinder-area-of-effect-xphb.md), [Emanations](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md), and [Spheres](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) created by your Warmage cantrips.
