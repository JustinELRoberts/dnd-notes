# Cryo Bomb 


By replacing your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents with a sliver of ice-nine, you can change it into a Cryo Bomb. This [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals Cold damage instead of Fire damage and all of its damage dice are d8s. When you use the [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) Explode property, each creature within the explosion makes a Constitution saving throw, instead of Dexterity. A target hit by this [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) or that fails its saving throw against it has a -3 penalty to its attack rolls until the start of your next turn.
