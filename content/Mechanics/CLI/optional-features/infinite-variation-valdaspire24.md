# Infinite Variation

_**Prerequisites**_ [Prestidigitation](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/prestidigitation-xphb.md) cantrip

You have become exceptionally skilled at using the [Prestidigitation](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/prestidigitation-xphb.md) cantrip to mimic other spells. When you cast [Prestidigitation](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/prestidigitation-xphb.md), you can attempt to emulate the effects of another cantrip that doesn't deal damage, even one that is not on the Warmage spell list. To do so, you must succeed on a DC 15 Intelligence ([Arcana](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Arcana)) check, otherwise the spell fails. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for a cantrip cast using this trick, and it counts as a Warmage cantrip for you.
