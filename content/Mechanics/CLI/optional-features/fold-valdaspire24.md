# Fold 


***Prerequisites*** Level 10 Warmage (House of Cards)

You have learned to graciously accept defeat using your Deck of Fate. As a [Reaction](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) when you are hit by an attack, you can play your entire hand to cast [Shield](Mechanics/CLI/spells/shield-xphb.md) without expending a spell slot.

Once you use this trick, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
