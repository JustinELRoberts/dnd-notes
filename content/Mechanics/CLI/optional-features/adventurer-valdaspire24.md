# Adventurer 


You picked up your magic informally by traveling with a dozen different mages over the years. You learn the [Mage Hand](Mechanics/CLI/spells/mage-hand-xphb.md) and [Ray of Frost](Mechanics/CLI/spells/ray-of-frost-xphb.md) cantrips and you gain proficiency in the [Perception](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Perception) skill.
