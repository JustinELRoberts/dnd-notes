# Acid Bomb 


By mixing your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents with a few drops of ardenflotic acid, a highly corrosive chemical, you can change it into an Acid Bomb. This [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals Acid damage instead of Fire damage and all of its damage dice are d8s. A target hit by this [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) or that fails its saving throw against it has a -3 penalty to its AC until the start of your next turn.
