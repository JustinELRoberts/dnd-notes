# Rapid Fortification 


***Prerequisites*** [Mending](Mechanics/CLI/spells/mending-xphb.md) cantrip

You can cast the [Mending](Mechanics/CLI/spells/mending-xphb.md) cantrip as an action or a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md). If you cast it as an action, you can use one of the following effects.

## Repair

You can restore a single nonmagical object, such as a door, [Cart](Mechanics/CLI/items/cart-xphb.md), or window, to pristine condition if at least half of its parts are present. This object can be no larger than 10 cubic feet, or 1 cubic foot if it is exceptionally complex (such as a clock).

## Fortify

You can build simple fortifications, such as sealing a door shut, adding wooden planks to a window, or building a short stone wall (no larger than 10 cubic feet). You must have the materials present to use this ability.
