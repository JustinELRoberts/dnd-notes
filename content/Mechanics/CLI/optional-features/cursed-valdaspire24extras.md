# Cursed 


You have been cursed by an item that you must always keep at your side. You have one of the following magic items of your choice: Cloak of Elvenkind, Cloak of Protection, Gloves of Swimming and Climbing, Helm of Telepathy, Ring of Jumping, or Stone of Good Luck. You might have a different item of the GM's choice. If this item is more than 5 feet away from yourself, you have [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on [D20 Tests](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md).
