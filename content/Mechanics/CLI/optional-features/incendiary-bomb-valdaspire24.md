# Incendiary Bomb 


By adding a thickening agent to your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents, you can change it into an Incendiary Bomb. All of this [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) damage dice are d8s. When you use the [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) Explode property, the area of the explosion is filled with flames until the start of your next turn. Whenever a creature enters the area or ends its turn there, it must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take Fire damage equal to the [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) damage dice.
