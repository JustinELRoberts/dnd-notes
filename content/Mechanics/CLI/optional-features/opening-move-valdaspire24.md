# Opening Move 


***Prerequisites*** House of Bishops, House of Go, House of Kings, House of Knights, House of Pawns, or House of Rooks

During the first round of combat, the first spell attack you make has [Advantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md). You can't use this trick if you have [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on your [Initiative](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) roll.
