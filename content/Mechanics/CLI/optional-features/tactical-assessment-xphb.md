# Tactical Assessment

When you make an Intelligence ([History](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#History) or [Investigation](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Investigation)) check or a Wisdom ([Insight](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Insight)) check, you can expend one Superiority Die and add that die to the ability check.
