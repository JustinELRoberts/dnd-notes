# Parry

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 2 Craftsman, Melee Weapon that Doesn't Have the Two-Handed Property

When you hit a creature with this weapon and you aren't wielding a [Shield](/Mechanics/CLI/items/shield-xphb.md), you gain a +2 bonus to your [Armor Class](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) until the start of your next turn. You can gain this bonus only once per turn.
