# Seeking Bomb 


By replacing your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents with a powerful lodestone, you can change it into a Seeking Bomb. Your ranged attack with this [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) and its explosion ignores [Half Cover](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cover-xphb.md) and [Three-Quarters Cover](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cover-xphb.md).
