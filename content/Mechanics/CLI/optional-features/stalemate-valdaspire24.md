# Stalemate

When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend one Battle Die as a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to hold that creature in place. Add the Battle Die to the attack's damage roll. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or its [Speed](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) becomes 0 until the end of its next turn.

_Valda's Spire of Secrets (2024)_
