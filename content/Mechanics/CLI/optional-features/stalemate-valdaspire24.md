# Stalemate 


When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend one Battle Die as a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to hold that creature in place. Add the Battle Die to the attack's damage roll. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or its [Speed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) becomes 0 until the end of its next turn.
