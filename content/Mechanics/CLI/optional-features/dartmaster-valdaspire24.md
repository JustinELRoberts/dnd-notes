# Dartmaster 


***Prerequisites*** Level 10 Warmage (House of Darts)

When you cast a cantrip that allows you to make multiple ranged spell attacks, such as [Magic Daggers](Mechanics/CLI/spells/magic-daggers-valdaspire24.md) or [Force Dart](Mechanics/CLI/spells/force-dart-valdaspire24.md) using the [Split Fire](Mechanics/CLI/optional-features/split-fire-valdaspire24.md) trick, and you miss with one or more attacks, you can reroll all of your misses and must use the new rolls.

Once you use this trick, you can't do so again until you finish a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
