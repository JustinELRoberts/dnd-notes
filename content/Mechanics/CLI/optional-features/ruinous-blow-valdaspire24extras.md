# Ruinous Blow

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 5 Vagabond

When you hit a creature with an attack using a weapon or [Unarmed Strike](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md), you can expend two Battle Dice as a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to sunder its defenses. Add the Battle Dice to the attack's damage roll. The target has a -3 penalty to AC until the end of your next turn.
