# Chains of Carceri

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 15, Pact of the Chain

You can cast [hold monster](/Mechanics/CLI/spells/hold-monster-xphb.md) at will—targeting a celestial, fiend, or elemental—without expending a spell slot or material components. You must finish a long rest before you can use this invocation on the same creature again.
