# Blitz 


When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend one Battle Die as a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to maneuver one of your [Allies](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/ally-xphb.md). Add the Battle Die to the attack's damage roll. Choose an [Ally](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/ally-xphb.md) within 60 feet of yourself that can see or hear you. That [Ally](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/ally-xphb.md) can take a [Reaction](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to move up to its [Speed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) without provoking [Opportunity Attacks](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack).
