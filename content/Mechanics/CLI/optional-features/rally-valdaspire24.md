# Rally

As a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) on your turn, you can expend one Battle Die to choose one ally within 60 feet of yourself that can see or hear you. That creature regains [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the number rolled plus your Charisma modifier. You can't use this maneuver to heal a creature that has 0 [Hit Points](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md).
