# Heel-Cutter 


When you hit a creature with an attack using a Melee weapon or an [Unarmed Strike](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md), you can expend one Battle Die as a [Bonus Action](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to prevent the target from escaping. Add the Battle Die to the attack's damage roll. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or its [Speed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) becomes 0 until the end of its next turn.
