# Fugitive 


You are being hunted. You might be fleeing the authorities, a wayward assassin, or a supernatural force. Your [Speed](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) increases by 5 feet. Additionally, once per turn, you can give a creature [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on an [Opportunity Attacks](Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) it makes against you.
