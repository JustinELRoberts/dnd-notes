# Battle Edge

When you hit a creature with an attack using Strength or Dexterity, you can expend one Battle Die as a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to improve the damage roll. Add the Battle Die and your Strength or Dexterity modifier (whichever you don't already add, minimum of 1) to the attack's damage roll.
