# Spellstrike

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 10 Warmage; [Arc Blade](Mechanics/CLI/spells/arc-blade-valdaspire24.md) cantrip, [Burning Blade](Mechanics/CLI/spells/burning-blade-valdaspire24.md) cantrip, [Frigid Blade](Mechanics/CLI/spells/frigid-blade-valdaspire24.md) cantrip, or [True Strike](Mechanics/CLI/spells/true-strike-xphb.md) cantrip

Whenever you cast [Arc Blade](Mechanics/CLI/spells/arc-blade-valdaspire24.md), [Burning Blade](Mechanics/CLI/spells/burning-blade-valdaspire24.md), [Frigid Blade](Mechanics/CLI/spells/frigid-blade-valdaspire24.md), or [True Strike](Mechanics/CLI/spells/true-strike-xphb.md), you can make two attacks as a part of the spell instead of one. You can only apply the cantrip's bonus damage to one of the attacks.
