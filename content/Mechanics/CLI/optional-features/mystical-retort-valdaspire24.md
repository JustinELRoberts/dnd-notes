# Mystical Retort

You learn the [Vicious Mockery](Mechanics/CLI/spells/vicious-mockery-xphb.md) cantrip. Additionally, when a creature you can see makes an attack roll against you, you take a [Reaction](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to cast [Vicious Mockery](Mechanics/CLI/spells/vicious-mockery-xphb.md) targeting the attacker. If the creature fails its saving throw, the attacker has [Disadvantage](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on the triggering attack roll, instead of its next attack roll, potentially causing the attack to miss.

Once you cast this cantrip as a [Reaction](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md), you can't do so again until you finish a [Short](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
