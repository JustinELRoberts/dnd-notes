# Stunning Blow

_**Prerequisites**_ Level 11 Vagabond

When you hit a creature with an attack using a weapon or [Unarmed Strike](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md), you can expend three Battle Dice as a [Bonus Action](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to attempt to stun the target. Add the Battle Dice to the attack's damage roll. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have the [Stunned](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Stunned) condition for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target repeats the save, ending the condition on itself on a success.
