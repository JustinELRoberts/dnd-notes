# Lightning Reflexes

When you roll [Initiative](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md), you can expend one Battle Die immediately after regaining your Battle Dice. Add the Battle Die to your [Initiative](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) roll. You can't use this maneuver if you have the [Incapacitated](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition.
