# Hideous

Your appearance is ghastly to behold. You have proficiency in the [Intimidation](/Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Intimidation) skill. When you roll [Initiative](/Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md), you can choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself to scare. That creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC or have the [Frightened](/Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Frightened) condition until the end of its turn.
