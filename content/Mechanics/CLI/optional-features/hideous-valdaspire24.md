# Hideous 


Your appearance is ghastly to behold. You have proficiency in the [Intimidation](Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Intimidation) skill. When you roll [Initiative](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md), you can choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself to scare. That creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC or have the [Frightened](Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Frightened) condition until the end of its turn.
