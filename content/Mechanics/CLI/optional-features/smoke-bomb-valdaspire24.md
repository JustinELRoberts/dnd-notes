# Smoke Bomb 


By removing your [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) explosive contents entirely, you can change it into a Smoke Bomb. This [Bomb](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) deals no damage, but creates a [Sphere](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) of smoke that is [Heavily Obscured](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/heavily-obscured-xphb.md). The radius of this [Sphere](Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) is twice that of the [Bomb's](Mechanics/CLI/items/bomb-valdaspire24.md) Explode property. The smoke lasts for 1 minute or until a strong wind (such as one created by [Gust of Wind](Mechanics/CLI/spells/gust-of-wind-xphb.md)) disperses it.
