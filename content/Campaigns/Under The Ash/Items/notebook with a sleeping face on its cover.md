## Description
A notebook with a sleeping face on its cover. There are three riddles needed to open it:

1. I did not write this one fast enough :(
	- The answer is "death"
2. "Before the first question, I am there. After the last answer, I remain. Scholars chase me through dusty pages. Kings fear the shape I might take. Name me, and you think you understand me. Yet the moment you do… I become something else. What am I?"
	- The answer is "the unknown"
3. "I am the lie that keeps the living breathing, the whisper that defies the grave. I grow strongest when the world grows darkest, yet I cannot exist without despair. I have no weight, yet armies march because of me. I have no voice, yet the hopeless pray for me. Break me… and hearts fall silent. Keep me… and even the doomed will fight. What am I?"
	- The answer is "hope"

The pages of the notebook are blank until an artifact is placed inside (confirmed only with the [[Campaigns/Under The Ash/Items/Diamond of 20 Monsters]] so far, but it is unlikely this only works with one of the 12 artifacts).
