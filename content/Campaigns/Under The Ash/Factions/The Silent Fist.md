## Description

A prospering organization of elves in [[Campaigns/Under The Ash/Locations/Evermore/The Vel’Therin Expanse/index|The Vel’Therin Expanse]]. They seek to grow their hideouts, build their numbers, and expand beyond the expanse. They work for whoever pays the most; right now that is [[Thalan’dor]]. Their sigil is a pair of lips with a finger over it.
