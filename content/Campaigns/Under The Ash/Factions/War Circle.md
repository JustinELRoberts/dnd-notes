## Description

Five bloodlines in [[Campaigns/Under The Ash/Locations/Evermore/Gravethirst/index|Gravethirst]] who rule through vision, combat, and prophecy. They wear their scars with pride, for pain is a form of remembrance. Their wars are not for land but for memory - each duel a story retold so their ancestors do not fade.
