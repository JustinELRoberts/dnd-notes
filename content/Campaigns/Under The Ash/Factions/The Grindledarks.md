## Description

Grindledark Lords once hunted the [[20 Monsters]]. After those hunts were complete and the monsters locked away, [[The Silent Fist]] rose up within the [[Campaigns/Under The Ash/Locations/Evermore/The Vel’Therin Expanse/index|Vel’Therin Expanse]] and have since taken sweeping control under martial-law-like conditions.
