## Description

A frozen island where time slows and the silence feels as though it is watching.

- [[Eira-Kaldran]] rises from the glacier: a crystal palace that holds memory, magic, and truths too dangerous to speak
- [[The Council of the Cold Veil]] gathers here, preserving knowledge that should not exist in the world
- [[The Bleeding Glacier]] leaks strange power, and within it, a fragment of something ancient is believed to be sealed
- [[Darnholt’s Eye]] studies futures that may never come, while the island itself resists change and invention

This place does not forget, and what it remembers may one day be enough to break the world.
