## Description

A land stripped of life, where stone and fire replaced all that once grew

- [[Grumdur-Khaz]] stands unyielding, with [[Thuldarim]] carved into the mountain and lit by rivers of magma.
- [[Mount Varrak-Duun]] burns as the [[Crimson Eye]], believed to house a god, though some whisper it is something bound instead. The [[Heartforge]] has gone silent, and no flame now answers the call of those who shaped the world with iron.
- In [[Molgrin’s Deep]], echoes repeat things never said, and numbers are whispered that no one wishes to understand.

The [[dwarf]]s believe they hold back the darkness, but one of the locks they once guarded is already gone.
