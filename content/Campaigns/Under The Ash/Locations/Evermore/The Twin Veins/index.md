## Description

Two villages divided by the [[Arterin River]], bound by centuries of resentment:

1. [[Greythorne]]
2. [[Brindleharrow]]

The two sides trade at dawn and aim rifles at dusk. The river remembers more blood than rain. Yet both lands share the same curse: something beneath their fields stirs, old and hungry, waiting for the harvest that never ends.
