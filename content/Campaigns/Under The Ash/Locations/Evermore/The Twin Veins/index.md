## Description

Two villages divided by the [[Arterin River]], bound by centuries of resentment:

1. [[Greythorne]]
2. [[Brindleharrow]]

[[Dreamgrain]] fills the fields, a crop that grows only where fate has touched the land. The river divides more than territory, it divides belief, history, and blood that has yet to settle. Something beneath the soil remembers when [[The Unknown]] passed through this place, or perhaps never left. This land feeds the world, but it may also be feeding something else.
