## Description

A land of bone and ash where death is not feared, but honored and remembered.

- [[Ash’Torak]] stands within the skeleton of a fallen titan, ruled by the [[War Circle]] and the strength of bloodlines
- The [[Cradle of Cinders]] burns with the forging of souls
- The [[Gravewind Flats]] whisper the names of the forgotten
- At the [[Eye of Ul’Zar]] time fractures, and those who worship [[The Unknown]] gather in secret

[[The Unknown]] once walked this land, and something of it may still remain beneath the capital. If the war drums ever fall silent, memory will fade, and something long gone will rise once more.
