## Description

A shop with vibrant colors staffed by a [[tiefling]] woman. On one wall is an assortment of petals that, when consumed, provide various buffs on a d100 roll:

- 10: Permanently identifies common plants
- 18: Eats it; gains advantage on Stealth in natural terrain for the rest of the day
- 34: One reroll of a failed attack
- 40: If he makes a death save, it becomes an automatic critical success and he rises to 1 HP
- 54: +1 AC for the rest of the day
- 62: No damage the next time he would take fire damage
- 81: Can declare one critical hit on an attack
- 99: Everyone levels up
