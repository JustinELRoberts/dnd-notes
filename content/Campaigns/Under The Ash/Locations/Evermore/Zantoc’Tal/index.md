## Description

The Living World’s Heart. The jungle is alive in every sense—its vines remember, its rivers dream, its beasts pray in silence. Here reigns [[Empress Ixaya]], chosen by spirits and crowned in flame. Her city, [[Tal’Ochan]], spirals around the roots of trees so vast they pierce the clouds. The jungle is no mere land—it is a god, and its children serve it with blood and song. But the jungle’s pulse grows erratic. Deep within the [[Throats of the World]], three seals guard an ancient tomb: [[The Cradle of the Unknown]]. One seal hums now, faint but rising. Ghosts dance in the necropolis of [[Chotal’Huun]], and in [[Mazca’Tan]], a child draws maps of ruins no one living has seen.
