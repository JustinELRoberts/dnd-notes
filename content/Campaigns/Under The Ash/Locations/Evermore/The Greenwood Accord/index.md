## Description

A land where iron and nature stand side by side, held together by will, law, and careful balance.

- [[Caer Thalor]] rises at its heart
- [[Ironhart]] blazing as a city of steam, diplomacy, and quiet control
  - Deep beneath [[Ironhart]] a machine whispers prophecy, and no one agrees on whether it speaks truth or something else
- [[Brackenmill]] fuels the world with labor and trade
- [[Evershade]] smiles softly beneath secrets and shifting alliances
- [[The Emberlight]] teaches harmony between progress and nature, but not all believe that balance can be controlled

The forest was never empty, and the roots remember a darkness that was meant to stay buried
