## Description

A vast desert of shifting dunes and burning winds, said to be the final breath of a forgotten god.

- At its center stands [[Zar'Vasha]], sustained by the sacred waters of the [[Mirrorheart]]
- The [[Sapphire Circle]] rules through control of water, magic, and trade, guarding secrets beneath the surface of the lake
- The [[Mirrorheart]] reflects things that are not there, and some believe something sleeps beneath its endless depth

Sandstorms carry more than wind, and elementals have begun to move with purpose rather than instinct If the oasis is ever drained, it may reveal not just truth, but something the desert was meant to hide.
