## Description

Raelor grew up close to the land. Years tending animals and cultivating the earth rewarded him with patience and good health. He has a keen appreciation for nature's bounty alongside a healthy respect for nature's wrath. As a teen, emissaries of king [[Thorne Durell]] approached his father's farm and claimed it for their own, offering little in compensation. Instead of fighting, his father just gave in, and moved his mother and younger sister to the city. Because of this, he despises modern invention. He rebelled, thinking his father a coward, and when they moved to the city, he went off into the wilderness. It has been over a decade and he has not seen them since. Mostly living off the land, lending aid to farmers further afield when they needed food or on occasion being recruited to aid in passage through the world between cities.
