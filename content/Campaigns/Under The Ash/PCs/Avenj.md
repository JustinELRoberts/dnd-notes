## Description

Created by the [[Council of Cold Veil]] as a means of teaching the young students in the tower of [[Varnholt’s Eye]] about magic. He grew up learning and passing his knowledge down. Currently, he is away from [[Campaigns/Under The Ash/Locations/Evermore/Iskar’Thul/index|Iskar’Thul]] on an adventure to hone his craft in the real world. Through his travels, he works in local alchemist shops by day, and wanders the streets at night searching for new animation subjects. It's been 4 years since he was sent away. He was the first [[warforged-efa|Warforged]] made by the council that has made it to the exploratory stage of the “project” he was made for. Which means so far, he is the most successful warforged created by [[Eira-Kaldran]].
