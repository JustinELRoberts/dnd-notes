## Description
Worship the goddess [[Mechanics/CLI/deities/faerunian-selune-scag\|The Moonmaiden]]. He grew up in an orphanage where he was physically and mentally abused, then escaped and lived on the street. Came across some adventurers who helped him get into a temple, began worshipping [[Mechanics/CLI/deities/faerunian-selune-scag\|The Moonmaiden]], and now is going out to spread the word.
