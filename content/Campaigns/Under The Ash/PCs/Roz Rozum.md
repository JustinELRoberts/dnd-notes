## Description
Worship the goddess [[faerunian-selune-scag|The Moonmaiden]]. He grew up in an orphanage where he was physically and mentally abused, then escaped and lived on the street. Came across some adventurers who helped him get into a temple, began worshipping [[faerunian-selune-scag|The Moonmaiden]], and now is going out to spread the word.
