## Description

Yharov was exiled from his clan due to showing innate aptitude for the shadow arts and stealth, which was seen as an affront to the face-to-face combat traditional of [[goliath-xphb|Goliath]]s. He wandered and found himself at the [[House of One Hand Clapping]], a monastery of monks practicing shadow arts. The location is unknown, those who find the cloister do so by not searching for it. He was trained in the [[monk-way-of-shadow|Way of Shadow]], and taught the ancient and secretive practice of shadow weaving, infusing shadow into the fabric of items. He is still honing this ability. Monks of the House were much sought after as spies, assassins, etc. It is unknown how contracts were made, but coin exchanged hands. After becoming disillusioned with the secrecy and unknown true purpose of these contacts, Yharov left in search of his own path, continuing to develop him own practice and craft his own meaning behind his abilities.
